# Session Transcript: Scenario 04 - The Shadow Fleet

**System:** ZERO-SUM RPG: ECHOES OF THE NOW (Analog / Tabletop)
**GM:** Sarah
**Players:** 
*   **Mark** playing **Jax** (Netrunner)
*   **Chloe** playing **Kael** (Solo)
*   **David** playing **Vira** (Tech/Pilot)

**Props on Table:**
*   Green, Blue, and Red D6s
*   Physical Action Point (AP) Tokens (Poker chips)
*   Paper Clocks (Drawn on index cards)
*   Physical "Bleed Cards" Deck

---

## [00:00:00] Introduction & Setup

**GM (Sarah):** Alright everyone, grab your character sheets. I'm handing out your starting Action Point tokens. Three physical AP chips for each of you. 
*(Sound of poker chips clinking on the table)*

**GM (Sarah):** We are playing Scenario 04: The Shadow Fleet. Let me set the scene. You are in the flooded lower levels of Sector 7. A rusted, unregistered "shadow fleet" data-barge is drifting aimlessly. It’s broadcasting a massive payload of stolen, encrypted corporate blackmail data. Multiple corporate kill-teams are converging on it. Your mission: grapple aboard the derelict vessel in the middle of a torrential acid storm, hack the physical black-box servers to download the data, and escape before the barge is scuttled.

**David (Vira):** Acid storm? Oh great. Is my hover-skiff rated for that?

**GM (Sarah):** Barely. I'm drawing three paper clocks right now and putting them in the middle of the table. 
*   **Clock 1:** *Acid Storm Intensity* (4 segments)
*   **Clock 2:** *Corporate Kill-Team Arrival* (6 segments)
*   **Clock 3:** *Barge Scuttling Protocol* (8 segments - currently empty, hasn't started yet)

**Chloe (Kael):** I’m loading my heavy slug-thrower. If we're racing corporate kill-teams, I want armor-piercing rounds.

**GM (Sarah):** Noted. You're currently racing through the canals. The rain is a sickly neon green, sizzling against the canopy of Vira's skiff. Ahead, illuminated by lightning, is the massive, rusted hull of the data-barge. Let's start with the approach. Vira, you need to navigate the turbulent, acidic waves and get close enough for Kael to fire the grapple. 

## [00:05:22] Scene 1: The Approach

**David (Vira):** I'll reroute auxiliary power to the thrusters to push through the waves. I'm rolling my Piloting. That's 3 Blue D6s. I'm also using my custom thruster rig, so I'll add 1 Green D6 for advantage. 

**GM (Sarah):** The waves are violent, and the acid rain is blinding your sensors. I'm adding 2 Red D6s for the storm's interference. Remember, 5s and 6s on Blue/Green are successes. A 1 on a Red D6 cancels a success and adds a complication.

*(Sound of six dice rolling across the table)*

**David (Vira):** Okay, let's see. Blue dice: 2, 5, 6. Green die: 5. Red dice: 1, 4. 

**GM (Sarah):** That's three successes, but the 1 on the Red die cancels one. So, two net successes. It's enough to get you alongside the barge, but there's a complication. The acid rain eats through the skiff's sensor array. I'm ticking the *Acid Storm Intensity* clock. It's at 1 out of 4. David, hand me an AP token if you want to soak that damage, or take a Bleed Card for the ship.

**David (Vira):** The ship can take a hit. I'll draw a Bleed Card for the skiff. 
*(David draws from the Bleed Deck)*
**David (Vira):** "Corroded Manifold" - The skiff will be sluggish on the escape. -1 die to piloting for the rest of the session. Damn.

**GM (Sarah):** Kael, you're up. The skiff is bucking wildly beside the massive rusted hull of the barge. You need to fire the magnetic grapple and secure the line.

**Chloe (Kael):** I brace my boots against the gunwale. I'm tossing an AP token into the center to push myself—I want to make sure this hits. 
*(Sound of a poker chip sliding across the table)*

**GM (Sarah):** Okay, you get an extra Green D6. Roll your Heavy Weapons or Athletics. The storm is still raging, so 1 Red D6.

**Chloe (Kael):** Athletics. 4 Blue D6s, plus the 1 Green D6 from the AP token. 
*(Dice roll)*
**Chloe (Kael):** Blue: 3, 4, 5, 6. Green: 6. Red: 3. Three successes! 

**GM (Sarah):** Perfect shot. The magnetic grapple slams into the barge's upper gantry and locks tight. The winch whines as it pulls the skiff taut against the hull. You all scramble up the wet, slick line. 

## [00:14:10] Scene 2: Boarding the Barge

**GM (Sarah):** You haul yourselves onto the rusted deck. The acid rain is eating away at your protective coats. Ahead is the sealed blast door leading to the server room. However, you hear the whine of rotor blades in the distance. I'm ticking the *Corporate Kill-Team Arrival* clock. It's now 1 out of 6.

**Mark (Jax):** I plug my neural cable into the blast door's exterior access port. I need to crack this before the corps get here.

**GM (Sarah):** It's old military-grade ICE. Roll your Hacking. 3 Blue D6s. The system is degraded but heavily defended. 2 Red D6s.

**Mark (Jax):** I'm using my primary deck. I'll add 2 Green D6s for my hardware. 
*(Dice roll)*
**Mark (Jax):** Oof. Blue: 2, 3, 3. Green: 1, 4. Red: 1, 2. Zero successes, and a 1 on a Red die. 

**GM (Sarah):** The ICE flares back along your neural cable. It’s a feedback loop! You fail to open the door, and the feedback hits your nervous system. Draw a Bleed Card, Mark.

*(Mark draws a card)*
**Mark (Jax):** "Neural Singe." I lose my next action, and I take a -1 on my next Hacking roll.

**Chloe (Kael):** Move over, sparky. I'm blowing the door. I pull a thermite charge from my bandolier, slap it on the hinges, and ignite it.

**GM (Sarah):** It’s going to be loud. If you do this, I'm ticking the Kill-Team clock again, because the thermal bloom will be visible on their sensors.

**Chloe (Kael):** Do it. We don't have time. 

**GM (Sarah):** Tick. *Corporate Kill-Team Arrival* is at 2 out of 6. The thermite burns white-hot, slagging the hinges. Kael kicks the heavy door in. You are inside the cavernous server room. Rows of massive, ancient physical servers hum in the darkness. The black-box is glowing ominously at the far end.

## [00:22:45] Scene 3: The Black-Box Hack

**GM (Sarah):** As you step inside, the barge's internal security wakes up. Two heavy security auto-turrets drop from the ceiling, their red targeting lasers sweeping the room. Furthermore, the barge's automated voice announces: "UNAUTHORIZED ACCESS. SCUTTLE PROTOCOL INITIATED." 
I'm starting the *Barge Scuttling Protocol* clock. It ticks by 1 at the end of every round. It's an 8-segment clock.

**David (Vira):** I'll lay down covering fire on the turrets with my SMG while Jax gets to the black box! 

**Mark (Jax):** I sprint to the black box. My head is still ringing from the neural singe. 

**GM (Sarah):** Vira, roll to suppress. 3 Blue D6s, 1 Red D6 for the turrets firing back.

*(Dice roll)*
**David (Vira):** Blue: 4, 5, 5. Red: 2. Two successes! 

**GM (Sarah):** You manage to keep the turrets pinned, their armor-piercing rounds chewing up the floor instead of you. Jax, you are at the terminal. The scuttle protocol means the data is being wiped. You need to extract it fast. This is a complex hack. You need a total of 6 successes over the next few rounds to get the data.

**Mark (Jax):** I jack in. I'm spending an AP token to negate my "Neural Singe" penalty. 
*(Mark hands over an AP chip)*
**Mark (Jax):** Rolling Hacking. 4 Blue D6s. I'll risk pushing my deck to overclock, adding 2 Green D6s, but I know that adds a Red D6. 

**GM (Sarah):** Correct. So 4 Blue, 2 Green, 1 Red. 

*(Dice roll)*
**Mark (Jax):** Blue: 5, 6, 6, 2. Green: 5, 1. Red: 4. Four successes! 

**GM (Sarah):** You tear through the first firewall. The progress bar hits 66%. However, outside, the sound of rotors gets deafening. *Crash!* The skylight shatters as a corporate drop-pod slams into the deck above you. I'm ticking the *Kill-Team Arrival* clock by 2! It's at 4 out of 6. And the end of the round ticks the *Scuttling Protocol* to 1 out of 8.

**Chloe (Kael):** I'm setting up a choke point at the blast door we just blew. I have my heavy slug-thrower leveled at the stairs. "Hurry up, Jax!"

**GM (Sarah):** Two heavily armored corporate operatives in sleek black tactical gear drop down the stairs. They see Kael and immediately open fire with smart-rifles. Kael, you're defending. Roll Combat. 4 Blue D6s, 2 Red D6s because they have the high ground and smart-link weapons.

**Chloe (Kael):** I'm spending an AP token. Give me a Green D6 for my combat stims.
*(Hands over AP chip)*
*(Dice roll)*
**Chloe (Kael):** Blue: 1, 3, 5, 6. Green: 6. Red: 1, 5. 
Three successes, but a 1 on the Red die! 

**GM (Sarah):** Two net successes. You blast the first operative, your slug tearing through his armor, throwing him back up the stairs. But the complication hits: the second operative's smart-rounds ricochet off the walls and hit you in the shoulder. Take a Bleed Card, Kael.

*(Chloe draws a card)*
**Chloe (Kael):** "Flesh Wound." Blood is dripping down my arm. If I take another physical Bleed Card, I'm unconscious.

## [00:35:10] Scene 4: Escape

**GM (Sarah):** Next round. *Scuttle Protocol* ticks to 2 out of 8. The floor is starting to tilt. The barge is taking on water. Jax, you need 2 more successes to finish the download.

**Mark (Jax):** I'm slamming my keys. Come on! 4 Blue, 2 Green, 1 Red.
*(Dice roll)*
**Mark (Jax):** Blue: 1, 2, 4, 5. Green: 6, 6. Red: 3. Three successes! 

**GM (Sarah):** "DOWNLOAD COMPLETE." The physical drive pops out of the console. Jax, you grab it. 

**David (Vira):** "Everyone out! The ship is going down!" I run for the grapple line.

**GM (Sarah):** The corporate operative on the stairs throws a flashbang into the room. Kael, roll Agility to shield your eyes, 1 Red D6 for the sudden flash.

*(Dice roll)*
**Chloe (Kael):** 3 Blue: 2, 4, 4. Red: 1. Fail and a complication! 

**GM (Sarah):** The flashbang goes off. Kael, you are completely blinded. Draw a Bleed Card.

*(Chloe draws)*
**Chloe (Kael):** "Sensory Overload." -2 to all actions involving sight. Guys, I can't see the exit!

**Mark (Jax):** I've got you! I grab Kael's tactical harness and drag her towards the blast door. I spend my last AP token for an extra action to haul her.
*(Hands over final AP chip)*

**GM (Sarah):** You drag Kael out into the acid rain. The barge is groaning, listing heavily to port. Vira, you are at the grapple line. The skiff is below, tossing violently. You need to hold the line steady for Jax and Kael. 

**David (Vira):** I brace myself. 

**GM (Sarah):** Suddenly, a corporate gunship descends through the storm. Its spotlight hits you. *Kill-Team Arrival* clock is full (6/6). The gunship's auto-cannon spins up. You have seconds to get on the skiff and cut the line. 

**David (Vira):** I spend my last AP token! I slide down the line, hit the skiff deck, and immediately hit the emergency release for the magnetic grapple while gunning the engines!

**GM (Sarah):** It's a desperate escape. Roll Piloting. 3 Blue. Remember your "Corroded Manifold" penalty? -1 Blue die. So 2 Blue D6s. The gunship firing adds 2 Red D6s.

**David (Vira):** Oh man. 2 Blue, 2 Red. I need this.
*(Dice roll)*
**David (Vira):** Blue: 5, 6! Red: 3, 4! Two successes, no complications! 

**GM (Sarah):** The auto-cannon tears the barge's upper gantry to shreds just as the magnetic grapple releases. The skiff drops, hitting the acidic water hard, and shoots forward into the darkness. Jax and Kael tumble onto the deck, the encrypted data drive clutched tightly. Behind you, the barge groans and slips beneath the waves in a massive geyser of steam and bubbles. You fade into the neon smog of the lower levels. You survived. Session complete.

*(The players cheer and collapse back into their chairs)*
