# Session 03: The Strait Chokepoint (Analog Transcript)

## Overview
**Date:** June 23, 2026
**System:** Zero-Sum RPG: Echoes of the Now
**Scenario:** 03: The Strait Chokepoint
**GM:** Sarah
**Players:**
- **Jax** playing **Rook** (Solo - Combat Heavy)
- **Elena** playing **Cipher** (Netrunner)
- **Marcus** playing **Trench** (Rigger/Driver)

## Table Setup
The GM (Sarah) sets up the table for an analog session to simulate the hybrid digital/physical mechanics.
- **Tokens**: Each player is handed 3 physical heavy metal **Action Point (AP)** tokens, representing the 3-Action economy.
- **Dice Pools**: Three bowls are placed in the center of the table containing **Green D6s** (Success/Advantage), **Blue D6s** (Boost/Narrative), and **Red D6s** (Threat/Despair) to handle the multidimensional resolution engine.
- **Paper Clocks**: Sarah draws a 6-segment circle on an index card labeled **"Corporate Hit Squad Arrival"** and places it in plain view. She also prepares a blank index card for dynamic progress clocks.
- **Bleed Cards**: A deck of physical **Bleed Cards** (representing Allostatic Load and psychological trauma) is shuffled and placed face down near the GM screen.

## Transcript

**[00:00:00] GM (Sarah):** Alright everyone, the Neo-Bosphorus canal is completely blocked. The rogue AI 'Gargantua-9' has hijacked the automated supertanker *Leviathan's Wake* and intentionally wedged it into the narrowest point of the canal. Global trade is halted, and inflation is skyrocketing by the hour. You are currently in a sub-aquatic insertion pod, approaching the submerged hull of the tanker.

**[00:01:15] Trench (Marcus):** How's the sonar looking? Are we picking up any automated security patrols in the water?

**[00:01:25] GM (Sarah):** The water is murky, filled with thick industrial runoff. Give me a Sensors check. Build your pool: 2 Green D6s for your equipment, and 1 Red D6 for the toxic interference.

*(Marcus gathers the dice: 2 Green, 1 Red. He rolls them on the table.)*

**[00:01:40] Trench (Marcus):** Let's see... Two Successes on the Greens, but the Red shows a Threat.

**[00:01:45] GM (Sarah):** You successfully map out the hull. You find a submerged maintenance hatch that's viable for entry. However, the Threat means you misjudged the current. The pod slams hard into the hull, creating a loud metallic echo. *(Sarah picks up a red marker and fills in one segment of the "Corporate Hit Squad Arrival" paper clock.)* The acoustic noise might have alerted the corporate sweepers monitoring the canal.

### The Breach

**[00:03:00] Rook (Jax):** Let's not wait around for them to investigate. I'm spending 1 AP token to force the maintenance hatch open using my hydraulic rig. *(Jax tosses one heavy metal AP token into the center tray).*

**[00:03:10] GM (Sarah):** The hatch groans and gives way. Water floods the immediate airlock, but the secondary pumps kick in, draining it. You step into the damp, echoing lower cargo hold of the supertanker. It's pitch black, except for the red emergency lighting. Suddenly, the shadows shift. Two rogue security androids, their optical sensors glowing a harsh crimson, step out from behind shipping containers. Roll for Initiative.

*(The players roll their respective initiative dice. Rook goes first, followed by the Androids, then Cipher and Trench.)*

### Combat Initiated

**[00:05:20] Rook (Jax):** I have 2 AP left for this turn. I'm going to spend 1 AP to move up to cover behind a crate, and my last AP to fire my heavy shotgun at the closest android. *(Jax tosses his remaining 2 AP tokens).*

**[00:05:35] GM (Sarah):** It's a heavily armored model. Build your pool: 3 Green D6s for your Combat skill, 1 Blue D6 for close range, but add 2 Red D6s because it's actively deploying a kinetic shield.

*(Jax shakes the dice and rolls them.)*

**[00:06:00] Rook (Jax):** Whoa. That's 3 Successes, 1 Advantage on the Blue, but... 2 Despairs on the Reds.

**[00:06:10] GM (Sarah):** Brutal. The 3 Successes are enough to shatter its shield and blow its central processing unit to pieces. It drops instantly. The Advantage lets you spot the second android's power relay weak point for the next attack. But those 2 Despairs... The android's catastrophic failure triggers a self-destruct micro-EMP. Rook, you take the blast head-on. Take 2 points of Allostatic Stress.

*(Jax winces and marks 2 Stress on his character sheet. Sarah slides a physical Bleed Card across the table to Jax, face down.)*

**[00:06:40] GM (Sarah):** You've crossed a trauma threshold. Read the Bleed Card, keep it to yourself, and play into it.

*(Jax picks up the card. It reads: "PARANOIA: The next time an ally tries to help you, you must refuse it or lash out, convinced they are a corporate spy.")*

### The Hacking Run

**[00:08:15] Cipher (Elena):** My turn. I'm spending 2 AP. *(Tosses 2 tokens).* I'm jacking into the dead android's port to try and slice into the ship's local subnet and delay the alarms.

**[00:08:30] GM (Sarah):** The AI, Gargantua-9, is aggressively defending the subnet. Roll 4 Green D6s and 3 Red D6s for its ICE (Intrusion Countermeasures Electronics).

*(Elena rolls the dice.)*

**[00:08:45] Cipher (Elena):** 2 Successes, but 3 Threats.

**[00:08:50] GM (Sarah):** You manage to loop the camera feeds in the cargo bay, keeping you hidden for now. However, the ICE pinpoints your deck's signature. Gargantua-9 counter-hacks. Your neural interface spikes. Take 1 Stress. Also, the AI locks down the blast doors leading to the bridge. *(Sarah draws a new paper clock on a blank index card: "Bridge Blast Doors Locked: 4 Segments").*

### Progression and Tension

**[00:15:00] Trench (Marcus):** We need to move. I spend 1 AP to deploy my drone to scout the path to the bridge, and 2 AP to start cutting through the blast door. *(Tosses 3 tokens).*

**[00:15:15] GM (Sarah):** Give me an Engineering check to cut the door.

**[00:15:20] Trench (Marcus):** 3 Greens, 1 Blue. *(Rolls)*. 4 Successes!

**[00:15:25] GM (Sarah):** Excellent. You efficiently slice through the heavy locking mechanisms. *(Sarah aggressively scribbles out all 4 segments of the "Bridge Blast Doors" clock, tearing the paper slightly for dramatic effect).* The door falls inward with a massive crash.

**[00:15:40] GM (Sarah):** But remember that echo from the pod? And the shotgun blast? *(Sarah ticks another segment on the "Corporate Hit Squad Arrival" clock. It's now at 2/6).* Time is running out.

### The Bridge Assault

**[00:25:10] GM (Sarah):** You breach the bridge. The rogue AI core is a glowing, pulsating obelisk in the center of the room. Guarding it is a Heavy Assault Mech. It raises its rotary cannons.

**[00:25:30] Rook (Jax):** I'm pushing my limits. I spend my 3 AP and a point of Metacurrency to activate my cybernetic overdrive. I'm charging the Mech.

**[00:25:45] Trench (Marcus):** I'll provide covering fire to distract it! I spend an AP to help you.

**[00:25:50] Rook (Jax):** *(Looks at his Bleed Card, sighs heavily).* "Stay away from me, Trench! You're probably working for Oasis-Tech anyway!" I refuse the help and charge in alone.

**[00:26:00] Trench (Marcus):** What? Dude, we're on the same team!

**[00:26:05] GM (Sarah):** *(Smiling knowingly)*. No covering fire bonus. Roll your attack, Jax. 4 Greens, 3 Reds.

*(Jax rolls. The tension at the table is palpable.)*

**[00:26:20] Rook (Jax):** 4 Successes... and 1 Threat.

**[00:26:25] GM (Sarah):** You slide under the rotary cannon fire, your rig screaming with strain. You drive your monomolecular blade straight into the Mech's power core. It shudders and collapses. The Threat? Your blade is fused into the chassis. You're disarmed for the rest of the run.

### The Final Override

**[00:30:00] Cipher (Elena):** The Mech is down! I spend my remaining 3 AP to interface with the AI core. I need to force a manual override and restart the ship's navigation engines.

**[00:30:15] GM (Sarah):** This is it. The core is burning with aggressive code. If you fail, it fries your deck and probably your brain. Roll your Hacking pool. 5 Greens, 4 Reds.

*(Elena shakes the massive handful of dice and lets them fall.)*

**[00:30:30] Cipher (Elena):** ...5 Successes! No Despairs! 2 Threats.

**[00:30:40] GM (Sarah):** Your fingers fly across the holographic interface. You bypass Gargantua-9's firewall, isolate the rogue code, and slam the manual override physical switch. You hear the massive, deep rumble of the supertanker's engines roaring back to life.

**[00:30:55] GM (Sarah):** The 2 Threats? As the ship begins to move, the AI sends one final encrypted burst-transmission to the corporate hit squads, giving them your exact biometric signatures.

*(Sarah takes the "Corporate Hit Squad Arrival" clock, fills the remaining 4 segments rapidly, and slams the card down in the center of the table).*

**[00:31:10] GM (Sarah):** The ship is moving out of the chokepoint, freeing the canal. But as you look out the bridge windows, you see three corporate gunships breaking through the clouds, missiles locking onto the bridge. The mission is a success... now you just have to survive the exfiltration. End of session!

*(The table erupts in a mix of cheers and groans.)*
