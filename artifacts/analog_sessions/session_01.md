# ZERO-SUM RPG Analog Session Transcript
## Scenario 01: The Memorandum Hijack

**Game Master (GM):** Sarah
**Players:** 
* Alex playing **Glitch** (Netrunner)
* Sam playing **Silas** (Face)
* Jamie playing **Vanguard** (Solo)

### Session Setup
**GM:** Alright everyone, welcome to Scenario 01: The Memorandum Hijack. On the table, I've placed the main objective card: "The MoU Server". We have our pools of physical Action Point (AP) tokens in the center. Everyone starts with 5 AP tokens. 
*Players take their 5 AP tokens from the center bowl.*
**GM:** I'm also drawing a 6-segment Paper Clock on this index card and placing it in the middle of the table. This is the "Orbital Station Peacekeeper Drone Alert" clock. If it fills up, the automated security goes lethal. Remember, if you take stress or damage, you'll be drawing from the physical Bleed Deck right here. 

### Phase 1: The Planning and Approach
**GM:** Oasis-Tech and Persian-Core are currently hammering out the final details of their Memorandum of Understanding on a neutral Swiss-styled orbital station. The independent smuggler coalition has paid you well. You are currently in a stealthed cargo pod approaching the station's maintenance bay. How do you want to breach?
**Jamie (Vanguard):** I'll spend 1 AP token to prep my breaching charge. *Jamie tosses a plastic AP token into the center bowl.* If we blow the outer airlock, can we get in before the sensors pick up the decompression?
**GM:** It's risky. That's a direct, loud assault. 
**Alex (Glitch):** Wait, let me try to slice the maintenance bay's proximity sensors first. I can create a spoofed signature so the bay doors just open for us without triggering the depressurization alarms.
**GM:** Okay, Glitch. That's a Tech roll. Because it's a corporate orbital station, the ICE is thick. Roll 2 Green D6s for your Netrunner skill, but you have to add 2 Red D6s for the corporate ICE.
**Alex:** Rolling... *clatter* I got a 5 and a 4 on the Green dice, and a 2 and a 6 on the Red dice. 
**GM:** The 6 on the Red die means a complication. You successfully spoof the sensors (the Green 5 beats the base difficulty of 4), but the Red 6 means the system logs a micro-anomaly. The bay doors hiss open, and your pod docks smoothly. However, I'm filling in 1 segment of the Peacekeeper Drone Alert clock. *GM colors in one slice of the paper clock.* 5 segments left.

### Phase 2: Infiltration & The Face
**GM:** You step out into the maintenance bay. It's sterile white, with zero-g lifters and several dormant maintenance drones. Through the heavy blast doors, you can hear the faint hum of the station's upper levels, where the diplomatic suites are located.
**Sam (Silas):** I'm going to spend 1 AP token to activate my "Diplomatic Immunity" social implant. *Sam tosses a token into the bowl.* I want to walk right out of the maintenance bay and into the main concourse, pretending to be a Persian-Core adjutant who got lost. I'll need Glitch to forge me a quick ID ping.
**Alex:** I spend 1 AP to generate the fake ping and broadcast it to your suit. *Tosses token.* 
**GM:** Silas, as you walk out, two heavily armored, white-chassis Peacekeeper Drones rotate their optical sensors toward you. "HALT. IDENTIFY." Roll your Charisma with 3 Green D6s, and 1 Blue D6 for the forged ping bonus. 1 Red D6 for the drones' baseline suspicion.
**Sam:** *Rolls dice.* Green: 3, 4, 6! Blue: 5. Red: 2. 
**GM:** Huge success. The Blue 5 and Green 6 completely overwhelm their suspicion. The drones' optical lights flash from yellow back to soft blue. "PROCEED, ADJUTANT." 
**Sam:** I tip my imaginary hat. "Thank you, units." I'm looking for the server room where the MoU is being drafted.
**GM:** You spot a secure data-spire in the center of the atrium. Glass walls, but heavy biometric locks. Inside, you see server stacks glowing gold, and two Oasis-Tech suits reviewing data pads. 
**Sam:** I comm the team. "Found the server. I'm going to create a distraction. Get ready to move." 

### Phase 3: The Slicing and The Breach
**GM:** Vanguard and Glitch, you are waiting in the maintenance hallway. Silas, what's your distraction?
**Sam:** I walk up to an automated synthetic-café unit and use 2 AP tokens to subtly override its thermal regulators, making it violently overheat and catch fire. 
**GM:** Oh wow, 2 AP for an auto-success. *Sam tosses 2 tokens.* The café unit begins spewing thick black smoke and alarms blare in the atrium. The two Oasis-Tech suits rush out of the data-spire to see what's going on, leaving the door cracked open for a split second!
**Jamie (Vanguard):** I'm moving in! I spend 1 AP for "Burst of Speed". *Tosses token.* I slide through the closing door of the data-spire just before it seals!
**GM:** You're in! But one of the Peacekeeper Drones spots your tactical gear. It raises its non-lethal suppression arm. "INTRUDER DETECTED." I'm adding 2 segments to the Alert Clock. *GM colors in two more segments. 3 out of 6 filled.*
**Alex (Glitch):** I need to get onto that server. I'm jacking in wirelessly from the hallway, using Vanguard's deck as a relay. 
**GM:** You're slicing the MoU contract before the digital ink dries. This is a massive cyber-warfare roll. 3 Red D6s. You have 3 AP left, Alex. 
**Alex:** I'm spending all 3 AP. *Tosses three tokens.* I want maximum processing power. 4 Green D6s, 2 Blue D6s. 
**GM:** Roll them.
**Alex:** *Rolls a massive handful of dice.* 
Green: 2, 4, 5, 5. 
Blue: 4, 6.
Red: 3, 5, 6!
**GM:** Oh man. The Blue 6 and Green 5s mean you successfully locate the exact file directory and begin extracting and corrupting the MoU. But that Red 6 means a Black-ICE countermeasure hits you hard. Alex, draw 1 physical Bleed Card.
*Alex reaches out to the Bleed Deck on the table and draws a card.*
**Alex:** I got "Neural Feedback: Lose your next action phase, take 1 point of permanent stress." *Alex places the card face up in front of them.*
**GM:** The Black-ICE fries your neural link temporarily. You slump against the wall, nose bleeding, but the data is secured. However, the Peacekeeper Drones in the atrium go fully hostile. I'm ticking the Alert Clock 2 more segments. *GM colors the clock.* It's at 5 out of 6! One more and they switch to lethal ammo.

### Phase 4: Escape and Extraction
**GM:** Vanguard, you are inside the spire, the data is sliced, but the door is locked from the outside and two Peacekeeper drones are bashing against the glass. Silas, you are in the atrium near the burning café. What do you do?
**Jamie (Vanguard):** I have 3 AP left. I'll spend 2 AP to use my "Kinetic Strike". *Tosses 2 tokens.* I'm going to punch the biometric lock console so hard it shatters, forcing the door to emergency-release.
**GM:** Roll 3 Green D6s and 1 Red D6. 
**Jamie:** *Rolls.* Green: 4, 5, 6! Red: 1. Perfect!
**GM:** The console explodes into sparks. The glass doors slide open. You sprint out. 
**Sam (Silas):** I have 2 AP left. I'll use 1 to grab Glitch from the hallway and drag them toward our cargo pod. *Tosses token.*
**GM:** Vanguard, you cover their retreat. But the drones are opening fire with suppression rounds. You need to roll evasion. 2 Green, 2 Red.
**Jamie:** *Rolls.* Green: 3, 4. Red: 5, 5. Uh oh.
**GM:** Both Red dice beat your Green dice. You take heavy suppression fire. Jamie, draw 2 Bleed Cards.
*Jamie draws two cards from the Bleed Deck.*
**Jamie:** "Shrapnel Wound: -1 to all physical rolls." and "Concussion: Discard 1 AP token." I'll discard my last token.
**GM:** You take a nasty hit to the shoulder from a ricochet, but you make it to the pod. Silas throws Glitch into the co-pilot seat. Vanguard, you hit the airlock seal. 
**GM:** I'm ticking the final segment of the Alert Clock. *GM colors the last slice.* The station's exterior defense turrets spin up, tracking your pod as it detaches. 
**Alex (Glitch):** I'm awake! I'll use the pod's countermeasures. I have no AP, but I'll roll base Tech. 2 Green D6s vs 2 Red D6s.
**Alex:** *Rolls.* Green: 5, 6! Red: 2, 4. 
**GM:** Amazing. You deploy a massive chaff cloud and spoof your engine signature just as the lethal turrets open fire. Tracers light up the void of space, but they miss your pod by inches. You vanish into the orbital debris field.
**GM:** You successfully sabotaged the Oasis-Tech and Persian-Core monopoly. The independent smugglers will be very happy. Mission Complete!

### Session Aftermath
* **Bleed Cards Drawn:** 3 (Neural Feedback, Shrapnel Wound, Concussion)
* **AP Tokens Spent:** Silas (4), Vanguard (5), Glitch (5)
* **Alert Clock:** Filled (6/6 segments)
* **Outcome:** Major Success with moderate physical/mental stress.
