# Session Transcript: Scenario 09 - The Ceasefire Backdoor

## Cast
*   **GM**
*   **Player 1 (Jax):** Netrunner
*   **Player 2 (Viper):** Solo (Heavy Weapons)
*   **Player 3 (Kael):** Techie (Drones & Demolitions)
*   **Player 4 (Lyra):** Face / Infiltrator

## Mechanics Used
*   **Dice:** Green (Advantage), Blue (Base), Red (Threat) D6s.
*   **Action Point (AP) Tokens:** Physical poker chips. Players start with 3 AP.
*   **Paper Clocks:** Drawn on index cards to track alert levels and progress.
*   **Bleed Cards:** Physical cards drawn when players take stress or critical damage, adding narrative complications.

---

## Part 1: The Briefing & Planning

**GM:** The neon glow of the Neo-Bosphorus flickers through the rain-streaked window of your safehouse. You're looking at a holographic projection of two PMC logos: *Aegis Vanguard* and *Crimson Syndicate*. They're signing a ceasefire tomorrow. Your fixer, a proxy for a munitions executive named 'Silver', slides a physical cred-stick across the table. *“Peace is bad for business,”* the proxy says. *“You are going to shatter this ceasefire. We need false-flag data planted on both PMC servers simultaneously. If the timestamps don't align perfectly, they'll know it was a third party.”* 

**Jax:** "Simultaneously? That means we have to split the party. Two to Aegis, two to Crimson."

**Viper:** "Aegis Vanguard's server hub is in an orbital drop-fortress downtown. Highly militarized. I'll take that one."

**Lyra:** "Crimson Syndicate is operating out of a luxury penthouse suite in the Golden Sector. High society, biometric security. I'll take Crimson. I can talk my way in."

**Kael:** "I'll go with Viper to Aegis. I can blow the physical servers if needed, and run interference with drones."

**Jax:** "Then I'm with Lyra. I'll slice the Syndicate's network from the inside while she distracts the VIPs."

**GM:** Alright, the plan is set. You are burning 1 AP token each for the prep phase. *[Players hand over 1 physical AP token]* I am starting two Paper Clocks. 
*   **Clock 1 (Aegis Base):** "Base Alert" - 0/6 segments.
*   **Clock 2 (Crimson Penthouse):** "Suspicion" - 0/6 segments.

---

## Part 2: Infiltration

### Team Crimson (Lyra & Jax)

**GM:** Lyra, you're wearing a high-end synth-silk gown, stepping out of an aero-cab at the Crimson Syndicate's penthouse gala. Jax, you're disguised as her bodyguard, carrying a concealed data-spike. Two heavily augmented bouncers block the gold-plated doors. "Invites," one grunts.

**Lyra:** I'm going to use my VIP forged credentials and drop the name of a known associate. "Tell Mr. Vance that Elena Rostova is here, and I don't wait in the rain." I'll roll Social.

**GM:** That's a Risky action. The bouncers have biometric scanners. Roll 2 Blue (Base) and 1 Red (Threat).

**Lyra:** *[Rolls 2 Blue, 1 Red]* I got a 5 and a 4 on Blue, and a 3 on Red! Success!

**GM:** The scanner blinks green. The bouncer steps aside. "Apologies, Ms. Rostova. Enjoy the gala." You are inside. Jax, you see the server room access down the hall, guarded by a single elite enforcer.

**Jax:** I need to get past him quietly. I'll spend 1 AP *[hands over token]* to deploy a micro-drone to short-circuit the lighting rig down the hall as a distraction.

**GM:** Roll Tech. You have an advantage because of the AP. 1 Green, 2 Blue.

**Jax:** *[Rolls 1 Green, 2 Blue]* Green is a 6! 

**GM:** Perfect. The lights explode in a shower of sparks. The enforcer draws his weapon and moves to investigate. The path to the server room is clear.

### Team Aegis (Viper & Kael)

**GM:** Viper and Kael, you are waist-deep in the acidic sludge of the sewers beneath the Aegis Vanguard orbital drop-fortress. Kael, you're patching into the maintenance hatch.

**Kael:** I'm using my plasma-torch to cut the magnetic locks. I want to do it fast.

**GM:** Fast means loud. If you fail, I tick the Base Alert clock. Roll 2 Blue, 1 Red.

**Kael:** *[Rolls]* Ugh. 2 on Blue, 2 on Blue. 5 on Red. That's a failure.

**GM:** The torch flares too hot, tripping a thermal sensor. The hatch opens, but an alarm klaxon blares in the corridor above. *[GM shades in 1 segment of the Aegis Base Paper Clock: 1/6]* 

**Viper:** "So much for stealth." I draw my heavy smart-shotgun and kick the hatch open all the way. What's waiting for us?

**GM:** Two Aegis security drones drop from the ceiling, their targeting lasers locking onto your chest. 

**Viper:** I spend 1 AP *[tosses token]* to get the drop on them. I'm blasting the nearest one with EMP rounds.

**GM:** Combat roll. 1 Green, 2 Blue, 1 Red.

**Viper:** *[Rolls]* Green 4, Blue 5, Blue 1, Red 2. Success!

**GM:** Your shot blows the drone into a shower of slag and sparking wires. Kael, the other drone is aiming at you.

**Kael:** I'll hack its IFF (Identify Friend/Foe) module with my wrist-comp! Roll 2 Blue.

**Kael:** *[Rolls]* 3 and 1. Failure.

**GM:** The drone fires. You take 2 points of Stress. Draw a Bleed Card.

**Kael:** *[Draws a physical Bleed Card from the deck]* Oh no. "Tremors." I lose 1 Green die on all fine-motor skill checks for the rest of the session. 

---

## Part 3: The Synchronization

**GM:** Both teams are at their respective server terminals. Jax, you've sliced into the Crimson mainframe. Kael, you've hardwired into the Aegis terminal. It's time for the synchronized upload. 

**Jax:** "Kael, you copy? I'm initiating the handshake. On three."

**Kael:** "Copy that. Ready on my end."

**GM:** This is a complex synchronized roll. Both of you must roll your Tech skills simultaneously. If one succeeds and the other fails, the timestamps won't match, and the mission is blown.

**Jax:** I'll spend my last AP *[hands over token]* for an Advantage. 1 Green, 2 Blue.

**Kael:** I have "Tremors" from my Bleed card, so I don't get any Green dice. I'll roll 2 Blue, 1 Red because the Aegis system is actively fighting me.

**GM:** Roll them.

**Jax:** *[Rolls]* Green 5, Blue 4, Blue 2. That's a success! My payload is streaming.

**Kael:** *[Rolls]* Blue 4, Blue 3, Red... 6. Failure! The ICE (Intrusion Countermeasures Electronics) is locking me out! 

**Viper:** "Kael, we don't have time! Reinforcements are coming!" I'm going to physically rip the server casing open to expose the raw data-bus so Kael can bypass the ICE! I'll roll Strength!

**GM:** That's desperate. 2 Blue, 2 Red.

**Viper:** *[Rolls]* Blue 6! Yes!

**GM:** Viper tears the titanium casing off with a grunt of exertion. Kael, the raw optical cables are exposed. You have one chance to jam the dataspike in before Jax's upload completes.

**Kael:** I'm doing it! 2 Blue! *[Rolls]* 5 and 4! Success!

**GM:** The dataspike locks in. The false-flag data floods both networks at the exact same millisecond. 

*   *Aegis Network:* "ALERT: MASSIVE CRIMSON SYNDICATE STRIKE DETECTED."
*   *Crimson Network:* "ALERT: PREEMPTIVE AEGIS VANGUARD BOMBARDMENT IMMINENT."

**GM:** The ceasefire is dead. Now you just have to survive the fallout.

---

## Part 4: The Escape

**GM:** The alarms in both buildings change from a steady hum to a frantic, wailing siren. The Paper Clocks for Alert and Suspicion are instantly filled to 6/6. 

**Lyra:** Jax, we need to walk out of here like we belong. I'll grab his arm. "Darling, the music is too loud, let's leave."

**GM:** The Crimson Enforcers are locking down the penthouse. One of them steps in front of you. "Nobody leaves. Security protocol."

**Lyra:** I use my last AP *[tosses token]*. I look him dead in the eye. "My father is on the board of directors, and if you keep me here during an active strike, he will have your cybernetics repossessed. Move."

**GM:** Intimidation. 1 Green, 2 Blue.

**Lyra:** *[Rolls]* Green 6! 

**GM:** The Enforcer pales, stepping aside. "Apologies, ma'am. Proceed to the elevator." You and Jax slip out just as the penthouse doors barricade shut behind you. 

**GM:** Meanwhile, Aegis Vanguard. Viper and Kael, you have a squad of heavy shock-troopers breaching the server room.

**Viper:** I throw my last frag grenade at the door and grab Kael. "Down the maintenance shaft!"

**GM:** Roll 2 Blue to cover your escape.

**Viper:** *[Rolls]* 4 and 5.

**GM:** The explosion shatters the doorway, blowing the shock-troopers back. You and Kael dive down the shaft, sliding back into the sewer sludge just as the orbital drop-fortress goes into full lockdown above you.

## Epilogue

**GM:** You rendezvous back at the safehouse. On the holovision, the news is breaking. *“Explosions reported at the Neo-Bosphorus border. The historic ceasefire between Aegis Vanguard and Crimson Syndicate has violently collapsed following mutual allegations of preemptive strikes.”*
Your comms chime. It's the proxy. *“Payment transferred. War is back on the menu. Good work, runners.”* 

**Session End.**
