# Session Transcript: Scenario 02 - The Synthetic Blockade

**Date:** June 23, 2026  
**System:** ZERO-SUM RPG: ECHOES OF THE NOW (Analog / Tabletop Version)  
**Game Master (GM):** Sarah  
**Players:**
- **Alex** playing *Torque* (Rigger/Driver)
- **Ben** playing *Jax* (Solo / Heavy Weapons)
- **Chloe** playing *Viper* (Solo / CQC & Demolitions)

---

## Part 1: Pre-Game Setup & Planning Phase

**GM (Sarah):** Alright, let's get started. We're running Scenario 02: The Synthetic Blockade. In the center of the table, I'm placing a fresh, shuffled deck of physical **Bleed Cards**. You each start with your standard pool of 5 physical **Action Point (AP) tokens**. Go ahead and grab those from the bowl.

**Alex:** *(Slides 5 poker chips across the mat)* Got 'em.

**Ben:** *(Counts out 5 red AP tokens)* Ready.

**Chloe:** *(Grabs 5 blue AP tokens)* Let's do this.

**GM:** I'm also putting out two empty paper clocks right now. *(Draws two circles on index cards)*. The first is a 4-segment clock labeled **"Border Security Alert"**. This fills if you cause suspicion at the gate. The second is a 6-segment clock labeled **"Minefield Navigation"**. This represents your progress through the No-Man's-Land.

Your crew is sitting in the cabin of a heavy-armored transport rigged with stealth plating. The back is loaded to the brim with synthetic rations and medical stims. Sector 4 has been entirely cut off by Omni-Corp, and they are trying to starve out the local resistance. You are currently parked two miles from the main border checkpoint. What's the plan?

**Chloe (Viper):** We have stealth plating, right? Can we just ghost through the checkpoint?

**Alex (Torque):** Stealth plating helps against sensor pings, not MK-IV eyeballs. If they do a physical inspection, they'll see the cargo. I say we bribe the shift commander. I've got 2,000 credits from our advance.

**Ben (Jax):** If the bribe goes south, I'm in the back with my heavy mag-rifle. I'll toss an AP token into the 'Overwatch' pool on my sheet just in case. 
*(Ben physically takes 1 AP token and places it on his character sheet's Overwatch square. He now has 4 AP left.)*

**GM:** Perfect. Torque, you're driving up to the gate. Viper, you're in the passenger seat?

**Chloe (Viper):** Actually, I'll be in the smuggler's compartment under the floorboards, ready to pop out if things get messy.

**GM:** Great. Torque, the transport rumbles toward the neon-lit checkpoint. Acid rain is slicking down the armored windshield. A heavily augmented Omni-Corp guard steps out into the floodlights, holding his palm up. He slaps the hood of your rig. "Engine off. Let's see the manifest."

---

## Part 2: Bribing the Border Guards

**Alex (Torque):** I power down the engine, but I keep the auxiliary battery running for a quick getaway. I roll down the window, sliding a forged manifest across, but under it, I've got a cred-stick loaded with 1,000 creds. "Just a load of industrial lubricant, officer. Rough weather tonight, huh? Maybe this covers a warm coffee."

**GM:** He checks the stick. Make a Manipulation roll. Because the stakes are high, you need 2 Successes. Roll your Green D6s for your Skill, plus any Blue D6s for your gear or perks.

**Alex:** I have a 3 in Manipulation, so that's 3 Green D6s. I'll spend 1 AP token to activate my "Smooth Operator" perk, giving me 1 bonus Blue D6. 
*(Alex tosses 1 AP token into the GM's tray. He now has 4 AP left. He rolls 4 dice: 3 Green, 1 Blue)*
**Roll Results:** Green (4, 2, 5), Blue (6). The 5 and 6 are successes. That's 2 Successes!

**GM:** Nice! The guard pockets the cred-stick smoothly. He starts to wave you through, but suddenly his shoulder radio crackles. A synthetic voice says, "Hold transport 44-Alpha. Random thermal sweep authorized." The guard looks at you, his cybernetic eyes narrowing. "Sorry buddy, protocol." 
I'm marking one segment on the **Border Security Alert** clock. *(GM shades in 1/4 of the circle with a marker)*. 

**Chloe (Viper):** He's going to use a thermal scanner? I'm under the floorboards! Can I dampen my heat signature or slow my heart rate?

**GM:** Yes, but it requires quick thinking. Give me a Stealth roll. 

**Chloe:** I roll 3 Green D6s. 
*(Chloe rolls: 1, 3, 4)* 
Ah, zero successes. Nothing above a 4.

**GM:** The guard's handheld scanner pings loudly. He frowns and rests his hand on his sidearm. "I'm reading a localized heat spike under the cab." Since you failed, I'm advancing the **Border Security Alert** clock to 2/4. He steps back and raises his weapon. "Step out of the vehicle, hands where I can see them!"

**Ben (Jax):** I'm spending my Overwatch AP token! I kick the heavy rear doors open and level my mag-rifle at him from the back of the truck. "Drop it, corpo!"

**GM:** This is a severe escalation! I'm drawing a **Bleed Card** for the crew because of the sudden spike in stress and panic. 
*(GM draws from the physical deck and flips it over on the table)* 
It's **"Adrenaline Tremors"**. For the rest of this scene, all precision and driving rolls require spending 1 additional AP token or taking a -1 penalty to all dice. 

**Ben (Jax):** Worth it. I roll Intimidation. 4 Green D6s.
*(Ben rolls: 5, 5, 6, 2)* 
Three successes! 

**GM:** He freezes, terrified of the massive gun in his face. He slowly lowers his sidearm. "Okay! Okay! Just go! I'm not dying for minimum wage!" 

**Alex (Torque):** I slam the accelerator! We're burning rubber out of the checkpoint and blasting straight into the No-Man's-Land.

---

## Part 3: Navigating the Minefield

**GM:** You crash through the barricade and into the ruined, muddy wasteland of the outskirts. The ground here is littered with old corporate UXO (Unexploded Ordnance). We are starting the **Minefield Navigation** paper clock (0/6). Every successful Driving maneuver fills a segment. Failures will result in taking structural damage.

**Alex (Torque):** I'm gripping the wheel. I have 4 AP tokens left. I'm going to spend 2 AP tokens right off the bat to overdrive the sensor array to spot the mines. 
*(Alex hands over 2 AP tokens)*

**GM:** Good move. However, because of the "Adrenaline Tremors" Bleed Card, you need to spend 1 extra AP to avoid the penalty, remember? 

**Alex:** Damn. Okay, handing over a 3rd AP token. I only have 1 left now. 

**GM:** Roll Driving. You need to hit successes to advance the clock. Since it's extremely hazardous terrain, I'm adding 2 **Red D6s (Danger Dice)** to your pool. If a Red D6 rolls a 1, you hit a mine regardless of your successes.

**Alex:** Rolling 4 Green D6s and 2 Red D6s. 
*(Alex rolls the dice pool: Green - 5, 6, 6, 3. Red - 4, 2)*
Three successes! And no 1s on the Red Danger Dice!

**GM:** Incredible driving! You swerve around massive craters, the heavy tires throwing mud into the air. I'm filling in 3 segments of the **Minefield Navigation** clock (3/6). 
However, the noise of your massive engine has attracted attention. In your rearview mirror, Torque, you see two Omni-Corp Interceptor Buggies cresting the hill behind you. They're mounting roof auto-turrets. 

**Chloe (Viper):** I'm climbing out of the smuggler's compartment and heading up to the roof hatch. I've got my magnetic explosive charges ready. 

**Ben (Jax):** I'm bracing against the back doors, laying down suppression fire. I'll spend 1 AP token to Brace, giving me cover. *(Ben pushes 1 AP token forward, leaving him with 3)*.

---

## Part 4: Corporate Interceptors

**GM:** The interceptors are closing in fast. They open fire! The transport shudders as heavy caliber rounds ping off your stealth plating. Jax, they are firing at the exposed rear doors. I'm rolling their attack: 3 Green D6s.
*(GM rolls: 5, 6, 1)*
Two successes. Since Jax is exposed laying down fire, he takes the hit unless he reacts.

**Ben (Jax):** I have my Heavy Sub-Dermal Armor perk. I'll take the hit, and my armor absorbs the physical damage. However, because I took a direct hit, I have to draw a **Bleed Card** for the concussive force.
*(Ben draws a card from the physical Bleed deck)*
Ah, **"Ringing Ears"**. I can't hear communications from the cab for the next two turns. I'm deafened.

**Chloe (Viper):** I'm up on the roof. I wait until one of the buggies tries to ram us from the side, and I'm going to jump onto it to plant a charge.

**GM:** That's a highly dangerous maneuver at these speeds. Give me an Acrobatics roll. 1 Red D6 is added for the high speed and slick rain.

**Chloe (Viper):** I spend 2 AP tokens to activate my pneumatic cyber-legs for extra jump distance, adding 2 Blue D6s. 
*(Chloe hands over 2 AP tokens, leaving her with 3. She gathers 3 Green, 2 Blue, 1 Red)*
*(Rolls: Green - 4, 6, 5. Blue - 6, 2. Red - 3)*
Four successes! And no 1 on the Danger Die!

**GM:** You leap gracefully from the roof of the swerving transport, soaring over the muddy gap and landing perfectly on the hood of the interceptor. The corporate driver panics, his eyes wide. You slap the magnetic charge right onto the windshield. 

**Chloe (Viper):** I jump back to our transport and hit the detonator! 

**GM:** The interceptor detonates in a massive blue-flame fireball, flipping end-over-end into the mud! *(GM crosses off one interceptor on her notepad)*. 
Torque, you still need to get through the rest of the minefield. The paper clock is still at 3/6. The remaining interceptor is right on your tail, pushing you into a narrow corridor of active mines. 

**Alex (Torque):** I'm out of AP tokens except for 1. I'll use it to slam the brakes, executing a sudden brake-check, hoping the interceptor overshoots us straight into the minefield! *(Slides his last AP token to the GM)*.

**GM:** Oh, a heavy brake-check! This is a Contested Driving roll. You roll your Driving, and I'll roll for the corporate driver's reaction time. 

**Alex:** I only have 3 Green D6s and no AP to boost it! 
*(Rolls: 5, 2, 1)*
Only 1 success!

**GM:** Let's see what the corpo gets. 
*(GM rolls 3 Green D6s behind the screen: 4, 3, 2)*
Zero successes! He completely fails to react in time! 

**GM:** You slam the air-brakes. The massive transport groans, tires locking up and plowing deep into the mud. The interceptor, going 90 miles an hour, swerves wildly to avoid rear-ending you and goes straight off the safe path. There is a deafening *KA-BOOM* as he runs directly over an anti-armor proximity mine. Dirt, fire, and scrap metal rain down on your windshield. 

**Ben (Jax):** *(Yelling over the ringing in his ears from the Bleed Card)* Did we get 'em?!

**Alex (Torque):** Yeah! Now let's finish this drive and get to the drop zone.

**GM:** Without the pressure of the interceptors, Torque carefully navigates the last stretch of the No-Man's-Land. I'm filling the remaining 3 segments of the **Minefield Navigation** clock (6/6). You're clear.

---

## Part 5: Arrival in Sector 4

**GM:** The mud finally gives way to cracked, ruined concrete. You pass through the rusted, broken gates of Sector 4. The neon signs here are flickering, and the streets are eerily quiet, lined with dilapidated megablocks. But as you pull your transport into the designated safehouse garage, the local resistance fighters emerge from the shadows. 
They look starved, battered, and desperate. But when Jax pops the back doors and reveals the towering crates of synthetic rations and medical stims, a wave of relief washes over them. 

**Alex (Torque):** I lean out the window, tapping the steering wheel. "Delivery for Sector 4. Sign here, please."

**GM:** The resistance leader steps forward, tears in his cybernetic eyes, and places a hand on the hood of your rig. "You just saved thousands of lives. The blockade is broken. The food flows again." 
And that's where we will end the one-shot! Great job, everyone. 

**Chloe:** That roof jump was so tense! I thought I was going to roll a 1 on that Red D6.
**Ben:** Yeah, those Bleed Cards really change the math on the fly. Being deafened made me completely useless for the second half of the fight!
**GM:** You guys played it perfectly. Let's tally up the final AP, clear the board, and pack up the tokens.
