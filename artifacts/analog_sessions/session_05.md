# Session Transcript: Scenario 05 - The Trillionaire's Ascension

**System:** ZERO-SUM RPG: ECHOES OF THE NOW
**Date:** June 23, 2026
**Players:** 
- **GM:** Game Master
- **Alex:** playing **Jax** (The Face)
- **Sam:** playing **Cipher** (The Netrunner)
- **Taylor:** playing **Rook** (The Solo)

## Phase 1: Setup & Planning

**GM:** Welcome to Neo-Angeles. Tonight we run Scenario 05: The Trillionaire's Ascension. CEO Elias Vance is hours away from an orbital IPO launch party at the hyper-secure Zenith Spire penthouse. You've been hired by a rival board member to infiltrate the launch, tap into the broadcast array, and splice in a Deepfake video that will crash his stock value in real-time.
*(The GM places a handful of physical metal Action Point (AP) tokens in the center of the table, sliding 3 tokens to each player.)*
**GM:** Here are your starting AP tokens. Spend them wisely. I'm also drawing two paper clocks. *(The GM sketches two circles on a piece of scratch paper).* 
**GM:** The first is a 6-segment clock labeled "IPO Launch". When this fills, Vance goes live and hits a trillion dollars, meaning you fail. The second is a 4-segment clock labeled "Security Alert". If this fills, the elite corporate kill-teams lock down the spire.

**Alex (Jax):** Alright, we need to get inside the penthouse. As the Face, I want to forge high-tier corporate IDs to get us on the VIP investor guest list so we can walk right in the front door.
**GM:** That's a bold move. It requires a difficult Face roll. 
**Alex:** I'll spend 1 AP token to gain an advantage on the forgery. *(Alex tosses a physical metal AP token into the GM's bowl, it clinks loudly.)* I'm rolling 2 Green D6s (Success) and 1 Blue D6 (Complication). 
*(Dice clatter on the table: Green 5, Green 4, Blue 2.)*
**Alex:** Success! My highest Green is a 5.
**GM:** The IDs are flawless. You get the VIP invites. However, the Blue 2 triggers a complication. The VIP IDs flag you as high-profile investors, meaning you'll be assigned an armed corporate security escort upon entry. 

**Sam (Cipher):** I absolutely cannot have a security escort looking over my shoulder if I'm going to slice the broadcast array. I'll sneak in through the spire's external maintenance shafts while you guys take the elevator. 
**GM:** Okay, Cipher, that's a risky stealth infiltration. Roll 1 Green D6 and 1 Red D6 (Danger).
*(Sam rolls: Green 3, Red 6.)*
**Sam:** Ouch. A failure on the Green, and a massive 6 on the Red Danger die.
**GM:** You manage to slip into the maintenance shaft, but you inadvertently trigger an active thermal sensor. *(The GM picks up a red marker and aggressively shades in one segment of the "Security Alert" clock. 3 segments remain.)* A patrol drone spots you. Take a Bleed Card.
*(The GM hands Sam a physical Bleed Card from the deck. Sam flips it over.)*
**Sam:** "Cyber-Static: -1 to all Netrunning rolls until rebooted." Great start.

## Phase 2: The Infiltration

**GM:** Jax and Rook, you step out of the golden elevator into the penthouse party. It's opulent—champagne waterfalls, zero-g dancers, and a massive holographic ticker counting down to the IPO. A heavily armored security android approaches you. "Welcome, honored guests. I am your assigned protection unit for the evening." *(The GM shades in one segment of the "IPO Launch" clock. 5 segments remain.)* Time is ticking.

**Taylor (Rook):** I hate this tin can. I'm going to discreetly attach a localized EMP limpet mine to the back of its neck while Jax distracts it.
**Alex (Jax):** I'll start talking up the Android about the champagne vintage. "Excuse me, unit, is this the '84 synthetic blend? It tastes a bit metallic." I'm rolling to distract. 1 Green, 1 Blue.
*(Alex rolls: Green 6, Blue 4.)*
**GM:** A perfect distraction. The android begins reciting the entire chemical composition of the champagne. Rook, roll to plant the EMP stealthily. 1 Green, 1 Red.
*(Taylor rolls: Green 4, Red 2.)*
**Taylor:** Success! The limpet is attached. I'll spend 1 AP token to arm it silently so there's no activation beep. *(Tosses an AP token into the bowl.)*

**GM:** The android remains oblivious. Meanwhile, Cipher, you're crawling through the vents above the party. You can see the broadcast array terminal through a grate in the server room. The IPO Launch clock ticks again. *(GM shades in the second segment of the IPO Launch clock. 4 segments remain.)* 

**Sam (Cipher):** I need to jack into that terminal right now. I'm deploying my cyber-deck cable through the grate. I have that -1 from my Bleed Card, though. I'll spend an AP to add a Green D6. *(Tosses an AP token).*
*(Sam rolls: Green 4, Green 2, Blue 5, Red 3. Adjusted highest Green is a 3 due to the Bleed Card penalty.)*
**Sam:** A 3 is a mixed success, right?
**GM:** Exactly. You splice your deck into the array, but the corporate ICE (Intrusion Countermeasures Electronics) detects a foreign hardware signature. *(GM shades in the second segment of the Security Alert clock. 2 segments left!)* Two security androids at the party suddenly stop their patrols and pivot directly towards the server room door. 

## Phase 3: The Broadcast Hack

**GM:** Jax, Rook, your android escort stops mid-sentence. "Anomaly detected in server sector. Please remain here for your safety." It draws a plasma rifle and starts moving towards the room where Cipher is hacking.
**Taylor (Rook):** I detonate the EMP limpet immediately! 
**GM:** Roll damage against the android. 2 Green D6s. 
*(Taylor rolls: Green 5, Green 6.)*
**GM:** Critical hit! The EMP goes off with a loud *CRACK*. The android sparks, seizes up violently, and collapses directly into a champagne fountain, shattering glass everywhere. Guests scream in terror. *(GM shades in the third segment of the Security Alert clock. 1 segment left until total lockdown!)* The other androids in the room immediately draw weapons and turn towards the commotion. 

**Alex (Jax):** I need to calm the crowd and cover for the explosion! I jump onto a hover-table. "Ladies and gentlemen, please! A round of applause for this stunning piece of performance art, commissioned by Mr. Vance himself to demonstrate the disruptive power of his new tech!" I'm spending my last AP token to sell this wild lie. 2 Green, 1 Red.
*(Alex tosses their final AP token. Rolls: Green 6, Green 1, Red 1.)*
**GM:** Unbelievable. A 6 on the Green and minimal Danger. The crowd pauses, murmurs, and then erupts into polite, wealthy applause. The remaining androids lower their weapons, their threat assessment protocols completely confused by the crowd's positive reaction. 

**GM:** Cipher, you have seconds to upload the Deepfake. Elias Vance is stepping up to the podium on the broadcast stage. The IPO Launch clock is at 5/6 segments! *(GM shades the 5th segment).* 
**Sam (Cipher):** I'm uploading the Deepfake now! I'm pushing my cyber-deck past its safety limits to force the data through the ICE. 2 Green D6s, 2 Red D6s!
*(Sam rolls: Green 5, Green 2, Red 5, Red 6.)*
**GM:** It's a success, but the double high Reds mean severe, localized feedback. You upload the video just as Vance begins to speak, but your cyber-deck violently overheats, sparking and fusing to your synthetic arm. Take another Bleed Card. 
*(GM hands Sam a Bleed Card. Sam reads it aloud: "Nerve Singe: Cannot use right arm. Take 1 physical damage.")*
**Sam:** Completely worth it. The payload is in.

**GM:** On the massive holographic screens above the party, Vance's face suddenly glitches and distorts. The Deepfake plays perfectly—crystal clear audio and video of Vance admitting to massive corporate embezzlement, funding shadow fleets, and intentionally ignoring structural failures in their orbital habitats. The live stock tickers floating in the room instantly flash from green to red. The numbers plummet in freefall. The room erupts into absolute chaos. The Trillionaire's Ascension is ruined.

## Phase 4: Extraction

**GM:** The Deepfake played, but the building's central AI just realized what happened. *(GM aggressively fills the final segment of the Security Alert clock.)* Total lockdown initiated. Heavy blast doors are slamming shut over the exits. Elite corporate kill-teams are dropping from stealth choppers onto the penthouse roof.

**Taylor (Rook):** I sprint towards the reinforced glass window overlooking the city and kick a hole straight through it! "Time to go!" I deploy my auto-grapple line. 
**Alex (Jax):** I'm diving out right behind you! 
**Sam (Cipher):** I have to manually rip my fried deck from the terminal! My arm is literally smoking. 
**GM:** Cipher, roll to extract yourself before the kill-team breaches the server room door. 1 Green, 1 Blue, 1 Red. 
*(Sam rolls: Green 2, Blue 6, Red 5.)*
**Sam:** Oh no. Failure and high danger.
**GM:** The server room door is blown off its hinges. Corporate solos pour in with tactical lasers sweeping the room. You manage to rip your arm free from the melted terminal, but you take a burst of sub-machine gun fire as you dive into the vents. Take two Bleed Cards. 
*(Sam draws two physical cards. "Shattered Ribs: -1 to Physical actions" and "Blood Loss: Take 1 damage per turn until patched".)*

**Taylor (Rook):** I'm throwing a flashbang back into the room to cover Cipher's escape! I'm spending my last AP! *(Tosses token).*
**GM:** No roll needed for the flashbang, the AP guarantees it blinds them just long enough. Cipher, you tumble out of the vent and dive out the shattered window just as Rook catches you with the grapple line. The three of you swing out over the neon-lit, rain-slicked abyss of Neo-Angeles, leaving Elias Vance's shattered empire—and his plunging stock price—behind you.

**GM:** Mission successful. The stock crashed by 82% before trading was suspended. Your fixer just transferred the credits to your untraceable accounts. Let's do the debrief and see about patching Cipher up.
