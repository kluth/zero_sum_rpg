# Zero-Sum RPG: Scenario 06 - Summit Sabotage
## Analog Session Transcript

**Session Date:** 2026-06-23
**Scenario:** 06 - Summit Sabotage
**Players:** 3 + 1 GM
- **GM:** The Game Master
- **Alex:** Playing **Jax**, the Netrunner
- **Sarah:** Playing **Kira**, the Stealth Operative (Prowler)
- **Marcus:** Playing **Vance**, the Face

---

### Game Setup
**GM:** Alright everyone, welcome to the table. Tonight we are running Scenario 06: Summit Sabotage. I've placed the physical **Action Point (AP) tokens** in the center of the table. You each start with your standard 3 AP tokens. Go ahead and take them.
**Alex:** *Grabs 3 physical AP tokens.*
**Sarah:** *Takes 3 AP tokens.*
**Marcus:** *Takes 3 AP tokens.* Ready.
**GM:** I am also drawing two **Paper Clocks** on these index cards and placing them right here in the center of the table where you can all see them.
1. **The Regulatory Vote Clock:** It has 6 segments. When this fills, the terraforming vote concludes upstairs. If you haven't broadcasted the un-redacted telemetry logs by then, Geo-Corp wins, their toxic dumping remains hidden, and the mission fails. I will tick this clock every time you take a prolonged action or face a major delay.
2. **Facility Alert Clock:** It has 4 segments. If this fills, the facility goes into hard lockdown and releases the automated defense mechs.

### The Planning Phase
**GM:** Your crew is currently in the maintenance tunnels just outside the subterranean server cooling facility of the summit building. How do you want to handle the approach?
**Marcus:** As the Face, my cover identity is an HVAC inspector. I can walk right up to the main service gate and distract the guards. I'll convince them there's a critical coolant leak that needs my immediate attention.
**Sarah:** While Vance is doing that, I'll use my optical camo to slip through the ventilation shafts and drop directly into the server room. 
**Alex:** And I'll jack into the external maintenance port here in the tunnel to provide overwatch. I can manage the network, maybe delay the Alert clock if things go south, and prep the broadcast array for our data dump.
**GM:** Sounds like a solid infiltration plan. Let's execute. Marcus, you're up first. Hand over 1 AP token for the action.

### The Execution Phase

**Marcus:** *Slides 1 AP token into the GM's pool.* I walk up to the security checkpoint, flashing my fake Geo-Corp ID badge. "Hey guys, we have an anomalous thermal spike in sector 4. I need to get in there and vent the primary coolant line before the servers melt."
**GM:** The guard looks skeptical and scans your badge. I need a Persuasion roll. Grab your dice pool: 2 Green D6s for your skill level, 1 Blue D6 because you have a highly believable prop (the ID), and 1 Red D6 because this is a high-security area and tensions are high.
**Marcus:** *Rolls 4 dice (2 Green, 1 Blue, 1 Red).* Okay, I got a 5 and a 3 on the Greens, a 4 on the Blue, and a 2 on the Red.
**GM:** The 5 on the Green means it's a success! The 4 on the Blue means you gain an advantage. The guard nods. "Alright, make it quick, buddy." The advantage means he actually hands you a temporary access keycard for the inner doors so he doesn't have to escort you. He tosses it to you.
**Marcus:** Nice! I catch the keycard and casually walk inside.

**GM:** Sarah, you're navigating the ventilation shaft. It's filled with toxic anti-freeze mist used for the extreme cooling systems. Hand over 1 AP token.
**Sarah:** *Slides 1 AP token to the center.* I activate my rebreather and use my vibro-claws to quietly dismantle the fan grate leading down into the main server room.
**GM:** Roll for Stealth. 2 Green D6s for your agility, and 2 Red D6s because the shaft is heavily rigged with acoustic sensors.
**Sarah:** *Rolls 4 dice.* Oof. 2 and 1 on the Greens. But a 6 and a 5 on the Reds.
**GM:** Oh boy. That's a critical failure with major complications. You manage to cut the grate, but it slips from your grip and clangs loudly against the metal duct. The acoustic sensors trip immediately. I'm taking my marker and ticking the **Facility Alert Clock** by 2 segments. It is now at 2/4. Also, the sudden movement causes the toxic mist to surge past your rebreather. Draw a physical **Bleed Card** from the deck.
**Sarah:** *Draws a Bleed Card from the physical deck on the table.* "Noxious Inhalation: -1 to all physical rolls until healed. You cough up black phlegm." Great. I am through the grate, though.

**GM:** Alex, you're jacked into the maintenance port. 1 AP token.
**Alex:** *Slides 1 AP token.* I'm trying to aggressively suppress the alarm Kira just tripped before they lock down the server room doors.
**GM:** Roll for Hacking. 3 Green D6s, 1 Blue D6 for your custom cyber-deck, 1 Red D6 for the system's ICE.
**Alex:** *Rolls 5 dice.* A 6 on a Green! 5 on the Blue. 1 on the Red.
**GM:** Critical success! Not only do you suppress the alarm, erasing the alert from the guard's terminals, but I will actually erase 1 segment from the **Facility Alert Clock**, bringing it back down to 1/4. Plus, the advantage from your Blue die means you locate the exact server rack holding the un-redacted telemetry logs. It's Rack 42.

**GM:** Meanwhile, the regulatory summit is proceeding upstairs. I am ticking the **Regulatory Vote Clock** by 1 segment. It's now at 1/6. Marcus, you're inside the coolant antechamber. Sarah, you drop down into the server room. What do you do?

**Sarah:** I need to get to Rack 42. I have 2 AP tokens left. I'll spend 1 AP to sprint across the room using my optical camo to avoid the automated cameras.
**GM:** Roll Agility. 2 Greens, 1 Red. Remember your Bleed Card gives you a -1 penalty to the highest result of your Green dice.
**Sarah:** *Rolls 3 dice.* Green shows a 5 and a 4. Red shows a 3. The 5 becomes a 4 because of the Bleed Card.
**GM:** A 4 is a partial success. You make it to Rack 42, but your camo flickers due to the thick coolant mist in the air. A patrol drone spots the thermal anomaly and moves in to investigate your position.

**Alex:** I have 2 AP left. I spend 1 AP. I'm going to hack that patrol drone and force it to reboot before it spots Kira.
**GM:** Hacking roll. 2 Greens, 2 Reds. The drone has hardened military ICE.
**Alex:** *Rolls 4 dice.* Greens: 4, 3. Reds: 6, 4. 
**GM:** You successfully initiate the reboot sequence (partial success on the Green 4), but the Red 6 means ICE feedback violently strikes your nervous system. Draw a **Bleed Card**.
**Alex:** *Draws a Bleed Card.* "Neural Scorch: You cannot use Blue advantage dice for the rest of the scene. Your nose starts bleeding." Ouch.

**Marcus:** I have 2 AP left. I'll use 1 AP. I'm going to use the access keycard I got to open the server room door from the inside and let myself into the room where Kira is, bringing my tool bag which has a physical data-splicer.
**GM:** No roll needed since you have the keycard. You enter the room and see Kira at Rack 42, and a security drone currently sparking and rebooting.

**GM:** I am ticking the **Regulatory Vote Clock** again. It's at 2/6. The Chairman upstairs is beginning his opening remarks on the terraforming quotas. The vote is approaching.

**Sarah:** I have 1 AP left. I use it to plug Vance's data-splicer directly into Rack 42 so Jax can extract the logs remotely.
**GM:** Roll Tech. 1 Green, 1 Red.
**Sarah:** *Rolls 2 dice.* Green is a 6! Red is a 2.
**GM:** Perfect. The splicer is connected flawlessly. Jax, the floor is yours.

**Alex:** I have 1 AP left. I spend it. I'm downloading the un-redacted telemetry logs through the splicer and routing them directly to the summit's main presentation holoscreens upstairs.
**GM:** This is the big one. 3 Greens, 3 Reds. Geo-Corp's Black-ICE is actively defending this file. Remember, no Blue dice for you because of your Neural Scorch Bleed Card.
**Alex:** *Rolls 6 dice.* Greens: 5, 5, 2. Reds: 5, 3, 1.
**GM:** Two 5s on the Greens! That's a solid success. However, the 5 on the Red means a major complication occurs simultaneously. You download the logs and successfully initiate the broadcast, but the Black-ICE traces your physical location in the maintenance tunnel. The heavy bulkheads slam shut, locking you inside. 

**GM:** Upstairs, the logs hit the holoscreens. The Chairman is mid-sentence when the un-redacted data appears, clearly displaying Geo-Corp's toxic dumping in the slums. The room erupts into complete chaos. The **Regulatory Vote Clock** permanently halts at 2/6. The mission is a success! But... down below, I am filling the **Facility Alert Clock** to 4/4. Sirens start blaring. The server room doors lock, trapping Vance and Kira. Automated heavy mechs drop from the ceiling. 

**Marcus:** Oh man, we are completely out of AP!
**GM:** Exactly. The session ends here on a massive cliffhanger, shifting perfectly into next week's escape scenario. Everyone, hold onto your physical Bleed Cards for the next session. Great job today!
