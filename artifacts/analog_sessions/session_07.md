# Session Transcript: Scenario 07 - The Heatwave Prohibition

**Game System:** ZERO-SUM RPG: ECHOES OF THE NOW
**Scenario:** 07: The Heatwave Prohibition
**Game Master (GM):** Sarah
**Players:**
- **Alex** playing **Jax** (Rigger/Driver, piloting "The Fridge")
- **Ben** playing **Sasha** (Face/Smuggler, the fast talker)
- **Chloe** playing **Kael** (Techie/Hacker, managing the thermal cloaking)

---

## Pre-Game Setup

**Sarah (GM):** Alright everyone, grab your physical Action Point (AP) tokens. You each get your starting pool of 5 AP. I’m placing the physical Bleed Deck in the center of the table. Remember, if you push your luck or take severe complications, you’ll be drawing from here. 

*(Sarah draws a circle on a blank index card and divides it into four segments.)*

**Sarah (GM):** This is the **"Citywide Thermal Grid Alert"** clock. 4 segments. If it fills, the automated orbital heat-lasers lock onto your truck. 

*(She draws another circle on a second index card, dividing it into six segments.)*

**Sarah (GM):** And this is the **"Cargo Integrity"** clock. 6 segments. The ambient temperature outside is 120 degrees Fahrenheit and rising. If your cooling systems fail or you take too long, the ice melts, the synth-alcohol spoils, and your client at 'The Sub-Zero' club will flatline you. Let's begin.

## Scene 1: The Briefing & Planning

**Sarah (GM):** The subterranean garage is sweltering. The neon lights flicker, buzzing loudly. Your contact, a sweaty fixer named Vex, hands over a datapad with the route. "The Sub-Zero club is in Sector 4," he says. "You've got three tons of purified glacier ice and forty crates of Blue-Nova synth-alcohol. Do whatever it takes, but keep it frosty." 

**Ben (Sasha):** I slide an AP token forward to use my *Street Smarts* ability. Do I know any corrupt beat cops on the route who might let us slip by for a cut of the synth-alcohol?

**Sarah (GM):** Spend the AP. Roll a Green D6 for your stat, and a Blue D6 for the attempt. The heat makes everyone irritable, so add a Red D6 for complications.

*(Ben physically hands an AP token to the GM. He picks up 1 Green, 1 Blue, and 1 Red D6 and rolls them.)*

**Ben (Sasha):** Green is a 5! Blue is a 3, Red is a 2. 

**Sarah (GM):** Solid success. You know Officer Krenshaw is working Checkpoint Delta. He's greedy but predictable. If you toss him a crate of Blue-Nova, he’ll probably look the other way.

**Chloe (Kael):** While Sasha is doing that, I'm going to spend 1 AP to calibrate the thermal spoofer on the truck. We need to look like a standard waste-disposal vehicle on the thermal scanners. 

*(Chloe tosses an AP token to the center.)*

**Sarah (GM):** Roll 2 Blue D6s for your Tech skill, plus 1 Red D6 because the truck's cooling system is already redlining from the ambient heat.

*(Chloe rolls.)*

**Chloe (Kael):** I got a 6 on a Blue! But... a 5 on the Red.

**Sarah (GM):** You manage to rig the spoofer, masking your thermal signature. But the system is fragile. Draw a Bleed Card and place it face down on your character sheet. You don't know what the mechanical flaw is yet, but it’ll trigger if the spoofer takes damage.

*(Chloe draws a card from the physical Bleed Deck and places it face-down without looking.)*

## Scene 2: Hitting the Streets

**Sarah (GM):** Jax, you're behind the wheel. The heavy blast doors of the garage open, and the heat hits the windshield like a physical blow. The city is a hazy blur of orange smog. 

**Alex (Jax):** I'll ease the truck out, keeping our speed steady to avoid drawing attention. "Keep your eyes peeled, Kael. The scanners are your department."

**Sarah (GM):** As you cross into Sector 2, an automated PA system blares: *“UN-SANCTIONED WATER CONSUMPTION IS A CLASS-A FELONY. REPORT ALL VIOLATORS.”* Suddenly, Kael's terminal lights up. Two Thermal-Drones are descending from the smog layer, scanning vehicles.

**Chloe (Kael):** I'm diverting power from the headlights to the thermal spoofer! I'll spend 2 AP to push the system into overdrive.

*(Chloe moves two AP tokens.)*

**Sarah (GM):** Because you're pushing the system, you need to flip that Bleed Card you drew earlier.

*(Chloe flips the card. It reads: **"Coolant Leak: If activated, tick the Cargo Integrity clock twice."**)*

**Chloe (Kael):** Oh no... "Coolant Leak." 

**Sarah (GM):** The spoofer kicks into overdrive, blinding the drones to your signature. But a pipe bursts in the back. A hiss of super-cooled gas vents out. I'm ticking the **Cargo Integrity** clock twice. It's now at 2/6. The drones pass overhead, oblivious.

## Scene 3: The Checkpoint Shakedown

**Sarah (GM):** You approach Checkpoint Delta. Concrete barricades funnel traffic into a single lane. Heavily armored beat cops are scanning vehicles with handheld heat-wands. Officer Krenshaw spots you and saunters over, tapping his baton on your window.

**Alex (Jax):** I roll the window down just a crack. 

**Ben (Sasha):** I lean over from the passenger seat. "Krenshaw. Hot enough for you? We've got a delivery for the waste processing plant." I slide a cred-stick and a small sample vial of the Blue-Nova through the crack. "Maybe this will cool you down."

**Sarah (GM):** He sniffs the vial. "Blue-Nova. Rare stuff today." He looks at the back of the truck. "My scanner says you're running awfully cold for a waste truck. Gonna need to look inside." This is a tense negotiation. Sasha, roll 1 Green D6, 1 Blue D6, and 2 Red D6s because Krenshaw is on edge and his partner is watching.

*(Ben rolls the dice.)*

**Ben (Sasha):** Oof. Green is 2. Blue is 1. The Reds are 4 and 6. 

**Sarah (GM):** Failure with a major complication. Krenshaw pockets the vial but shakes his head. "Not enough. My partner's a stickler. I need a full crate, or you're both going to a sweat-camp." He reaches for his sidearm. I'm ticking the **Citywide Thermal Grid Alert** clock once. It's at 1/4. 

**Ben (Sasha):** "Fine, fine! One crate." I look at Jax. "Pop the back, but just enough to slide a crate out."

**Alex (Jax):** I spend 1 AP to quickly pop the hatch and toss a crate of Blue-Nova out before the ambient heat completely melts our stash. 

**Sarah (GM):** Because the hatch is opened in 120-degree heat, I'm ticking the **Cargo Integrity** clock again. It's at 3/6. Krenshaw grabs the crate and waves you through. 

## Scene 4: The Heat-Dome Ambush

**Sarah (GM):** You're in Sector 4. The destination is only three blocks away. Suddenly, an EMP proximity mine detonates near your front axle. A gang of scavengers, desperate for water, descends from the fire escapes! 

**Alex (Jax):** "Hang on!" I spend 2 AP to execute an evasive drift, trying to plow through their barricade without damaging the cargo hold.

*(Alex hands over 2 AP tokens.)*

**Sarah (GM):** Roll 2 Green D6s for your driving skill, 1 Blue D6, and 2 Red D6s for the scavengers and the damaged axle.

*(Alex rolls vigorously.)*

**Alex (Jax):** Green: 5 and 6! Blue: 4. Red: 2 and 3. Huge success!

**Sarah (GM):** The Fridge roars as Jax slams the accelerator. The reinforced grill smashes through the scavengers' barricade like it's made of paper. However, a scavenger manages to latch onto the side of the truck, ripping at the coolant pipes!

**Chloe (Kael):** I'm unbuckling and climbing onto the running board! I spend 1 AP and roll to physically kick him off before he does more damage. 1 Blue, 1 Red.

*(Chloe rolls.)*

**Chloe (Kael):** Blue is 4, Red is 5. 

**Sarah (GM):** You kick him off, but he rips a chunk of the exterior insulation with him. Take a Bleed Card for physical exertion in this heat, Kael. 

*(Chloe draws a Bleed Card: **"Heat Stroke: -1 to all physical rolls until hydrated."**)*

**Sarah (GM):** And I tick the **Cargo Integrity** clock one more time. It's at 4/6. Almost slush. 

## Scene 5: Arrival at The Sub-Zero

**Sarah (GM):** You tear down a ramp into an abandoned subway station. The blast doors to 'The Sub-Zero' club grind open. The truck screeches to a halt inside the heavily air-conditioned loading bay. 

**Alex (Jax):** I kill the engine and immediately check the cargo temp.

**Sarah (GM):** You open the back. The ice is heavily sweating, and the crates of Blue-Nova are warm to the touch, but they are intact. The club owner, a cyborg encased in frost-bitten armor, steps out. "Cutting it close, smugglers. But the merchandise is acceptable." 

**Ben (Sasha):** "We lost one crate to a toll booth, and Kael looks like she's about to pass out. We expect a bonus for the hassle."

**Sarah (GM):** The owner tosses you a cred-chip. "Consider it a hazard fee. Drink up, the heatwave outside is just getting started." Mission accomplished, crew. Let's count our remaining AP and convert it to experience points!

**[Session Ends]**
