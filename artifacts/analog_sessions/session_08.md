# Session 08: The Energy Buffer Hack
**System:** Zero-Sum RPG: Echoes of the Now
**Scenario:** 08 - The Energy Buffer Hack
**GM:** David
**Players:** 
- Alice playing **Jolt** (Techie)
- Bob playing **Synapse** (Netrunner)
- Charlie playing **Brick** (Solo)

## Mechanics in Play
- **Dice:** Green (Advantage/Skill), Blue (Standard/Attribute), Red (Danger/Stress) D6s.
- **Tokens:** Physical Action Point (AP) tokens used to take actions.
- **Clocks:** Paper clocks drawn on the table:
  - *Midnight Shutoff* (8 segments): When full, the slums lose power, and life-support systems fail.
  - *Security Alert* (6 segments): When full, elite corporate hit squads arrive.
- **Cards:** Physical Bleed Cards drawn when taking severe physical or mental damage.

---

## 1. Planning Phase

**GM (David):** Alright everyone, let's set the scene. It's 22:00. The rain is acidic and heavy. You are standing in the alleyway across from the Regional Grid Control Center. It's a brutalist concrete block humming with stolen energy. You have two hours in-game before the *Midnight Shutoff* clock fills up. I'm placing the paper clock in the middle of the table. It has 8 segments. Right next to it is the *Security Alert* clock, 6 segments. Both are currently empty.

**Alice (Jolt):** I check my gear. I have my custom hydro-spanner and a localized EMP grenade. How many AP tokens do we start with?

**GM:** Everyone take 4 AP tokens from the bowl.

*Alice, Bob, and Charlie each grab 4 plastic AP tokens.*

**Bob (Synapse):** We need a way in. I want to scan the building's local network for any vulnerabilities in the biometric locks at the front entrance. I'm spending 1 AP token.
*Bob tosses a token into the center tray.*

**GM:** Roll your Interface skill. The security is tight, so take 2 Blue dice and 1 Red danger die.

**Bob:** I'll use my "Neural-Link" implant to add 1 Green die. 
*Bob rolls 1 Green, 2 Blue, 1 Red.*
*Result: Green (5), Blue (4, 2), Red (3).*
Since my highest is the Green 5, it's a strong success. The Red is lower, so no complications!

**GM:** Perfect. You find a blind spot in the network. The front entrance biometric scanner is hardwired, but there's a maintenance hatch on the roof that relies on an older, crackable protocol. It’ll take some climbing.

**Charlie (Brick):** Climbing? I'm carrying heavy ordnance. I'll take point and haul Synapse up if he struggles. 

**Alice:** I’ll follow. Let's move to the roof.

## 2. Infiltration

**GM:** The three of you scale the rusted fire escape. The rain is slick. Charlie, I need an Athletics check to keep everyone steady. Spend an AP.

**Charlie:** *Tosses an AP token. He now has 3.*
Rolling 3 Blue dice for my raw Strength. I'll add a Red die because of the slippery acid rain.
*Rolls 3 Blue, 1 Red.*
*Result: Blue (6, 4, 1), Red (5).*

**GM:** A 6! You muscle everyone up the ladder, but that Red 5 means the acid rain takes a toll. Charlie, draw a Bleed Card.

*Charlie draws a Bleed Card from the physical deck and flips it over.*
**Charlie:** "Acid Burns. Take -1 to precision tasks until treated." Great. Good thing I'm just here to hit things.

**GM:** You reach the roof. Synapse, you're up. The maintenance hatch is locked down with a biometric seal. 

**Bob:** I'm spending another AP to jack into the hatch's port. I'm rolling 2 Green, 2 Blue, 1 Red.
*Rolls: Green (6, 3), Blue (2, 2), Red (6).*
Uh oh. A 6 on the Green, but a 6 on the Red.

**GM:** A critical success with a severe complication! You pop the hatch open, but the feedback fries a sub-routine. I'm filling one segment on the *Security Alert* clock. *David colors in a slice of the paper clock with a red marker.* Furthermore, the system initiates a trace. Synapse, you take a Bleed Card for mental strain.

*Bob draws a Bleed Card.*
**Bob:** "Neural Feedback. Discard 1 AP token." Ouch. *Bob tosses an AP token into the tray. He has 1 left.*

## 3. The Control Room

**GM:** You drop down into the facility. It’s cold and smells like ozone. You navigate the ventilation shafts and drop into the central distribution terminal room. There are two automated security mechs patrolling the floor. The terminal is on a raised platform.

**Alice:** We need to deal with the mechs before I can override the central terminal. I’ve only got 4 AP left.

**Charlie:** Leave the mechs to me. I'll spend 2 AP tokens to activate my Sandevistan speed-ware and charge the first mech with my mono-katana. 
*Charlie tosses 2 AP tokens into the tray. He has 1 left.*

**GM:** This is highly dangerous. Roll 2 Blue dice, plus 2 Red dice for the two mechs. 

**Charlie:** I'm using my "CQC Specialist" trait for 2 Green dice!
*Rolls: Green (5, 4), Blue (3, 1), Red (4, 2).*
Highest is a Green 5! I slice through the first mech's optic sensors.

**GM:** You cleave the first mech in half, sparks flying everywhere! But the second mech swivels its automated turret toward you. It fires!

**Alice:** I spend 1 AP to throw my localized EMP grenade at the second mech! *Alice tosses a token. She has 3 left.*
*Rolls: 1 Green, 2 Blue, 1 Red.*
*Result: Green (2), Blue (5, 4), Red (1).*
A 5!

**GM:** The EMP detonates! The second mech seizes up, its servos locking into place as the turret spins wildly out of control before shutting down. The room is clear, but the noise was loud. *David colors in a second segment on the Security Alert clock.*

## 4. The Override

**GM:** The terminal is clear. It's 23:45. The *Midnight Shutoff* clock automatically advances. *David colors in 4 segments of the Midnight Shutoff clock.* You have very little time left before the slums go dark. 

**Alice:** I run to the terminal. I'm spending 2 AP. *Tosses tokens. She has 1 left.*
I need to physically bypass the biometric locks on the terminal casing using my hydro-spanner, then wire it for Synapse to slice.

**GM:** This is complex engineering. 3 Blue dice, 1 Red die.

**Alice:** Rolling. 
*Rolls: Blue (4, 3, 2), Red (4).*
A tie between Blue and Red. A mixed success.

**GM:** You get the casing off, but you sever a primary coolant line in the process. The terminal starts overheating. I'm advancing the *Security Alert* clock by 1 segment. It's at 3/6.

**Bob:** My turn. I jack directly into the exposed terminal. I need to reroute the eroding energy buffers back to the slums. I have 1 AP left. I spend it.
*Bob tosses his last token.*

**GM:** This is the core system. It is fighting you tooth and nail. Roll 1 Blue die, 2 Red dice. If you fail, the system locks you out permanently, and the slums go dark.

**Bob:** This is it. 
*Rolls: 1 Blue, 2 Red.*
*Result: Blue (2), Red (5, 4).*
Oh no. 

**Charlie:** Wait! I can spend my last AP token to "Push" a teammate. I'll use my strength to manually hold the severed coolant line together, preventing the terminal from overheating long enough to give Synapse an edge. *Charlie tosses his last token.*

**GM:** Okay, Charlie, draw a Bleed Card for the freezing coolant burning your hands. 
*Charlie draws a Bleed Card.*
**Charlie:** "Frostbite. Drop any held weapons." My mono-katana clatters to the floor.

**GM:** Bob, Charlie's sacrifice gives you 2 Green dice. Reroll the whole pool.

**Bob:** Come on...
*Rolls: 2 Green, 1 Blue, 2 Red.*
*Result: Green (6, 4), Blue (3), Red (5, 2).*
YES! A 6 on Green!

## 5. The Escape

**GM:** Synapse, your code breaches the final firewall. On the massive screens above you, the grid map flashes. The red zones in the slums suddenly turn a vibrant, stable green. The energy buffers are rerouted. You did it.
But immediately, the sirens wail. *David colors in the final 3 segments of the Security Alert clock. It is full.* The heavy blast doors at the end of the hall blow open. A corporate hit squad pours in. 

**Alice:** We have no AP left!

**GM:** That's right. The session ends here, as you dive for the ventilation shafts, corporate laser fire scorching the walls behind you. The slums have power, but you are now the most wanted runners in the sector. Great job, everyone!

## Session Summary
- **Midnight Shutoff Clock:** 4/8 segments filled (Success).
- **Security Alert Clock:** 6/6 segments filled (Hit Squads arrived).
- **AP Tokens Remaining:** 0 for all players.
- **Bleed Cards Drawn:** Charlie (2 - Acid Burns, Frostbite), Bob (1 - Neural Feedback).
