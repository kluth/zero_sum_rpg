# Zero-Sum RPG: Echoes of the Now
## Session 10 Transcript: The Automated Distribution Heist

**Game Master (GM):** Alex
**Players:** 
- **Sarah** playing **Jax** (Techie / Netrunner)
- **Marcus** playing **Kael** (Solo / Heavy Weapons)
- **Chloe** playing **Lyra** (Combat Drone Rigger)

---

### Part 1: The Briefing & Planning

**GM:** "Alright everyone, grab your character sheets and let's set up the table. I'm placing the 'Corporate Alert' paper clock right in the middle of the table. It has 8 segments. Right now, it's at zero." *(Alex draws a circle on an index card and divides it into 8 pie slices).* "Here are your Action Point tokens for the session." *(Alex slides 3 red poker chips to each player).* "You each have 3 AP to start. Remember, once you're out, you're relying strictly on your base stats without bonuses until you take a Breather."

**Sarah (Jax):** "Got it. Who's our contact for this job?"

**GM:** "You're sitting in the back room of 'The Rusty Spoon', a makeshift soup kitchen in the lower levels. Across from you is Mama Z, a community leader. She looks exhausted. She slides a datapad across the table. 'Agri-Corp has a fully automated distribution hub three sectors over,' she says. 'They’re sitting on millions of tons of synthetic rations, waiting for the famine to drive prices up. We need those rations now. Break in, reprogram the cargo drones, and air-drop the food over Sector 4.'"

**Marcus (Kael):** "An automated hub? That means no human guards to bribe or intimidate. Just cold, hard steel."

**Chloe (Lyra):** "Which is perfect for us. I've got my spider-drones prepped. Jax, can you handle the mainframe?"

**Sarah (Jax):** "If I can get to a hardline terminal, absolutely. Do we have a layout?"

**GM:** "Mama Z provides a rough schematic." *(Alex sketches a quick map on a piece of grid paper).* "There's an outer blast door, an antechamber which usually houses automated turret defenses, and the central distribution bay where the control terminal is located. And probably a heavy security mech on standby."

**Marcus (Kael):** "I say we blow the blast doors and rush the antechamber."

**Sarah (Jax):** "No way, that'll immediately tick up the Alert clock. Let me slice the blast door quietly."

**Marcus (Kael):** "Fine, but if you fail, I'm using the thermite."

### Part 2: The Breach

**GM:** "You arrive at the Agri-Corp hub. The rain is pouring, neon reflecting off the slick plasteel walls. You're at the main blast doors. Jax, you're up."

**Sarah (Jax):** "I'll spend an Action Point token to give myself an edge on this hack." *(Sarah tosses one red chip into the center bowl. She has 2 AP left).* "I'm jacking my wrist-comp directly into the door's maintenance port. I'll roll 2 Green D6s for my Tech skill, 1 Blue D6 for my custom splicing gear, and... how many Red D6s for danger?"

**GM:** "It's a standard Agri-Corp firewall, so just 1 Red D6 for Danger. Roll 'em."

*(Clatter of dice on the table)*

**Sarah (Jax):** "Okay, on the Green D6s I got a 4 and a 5. On the Blue D6, a 6! But the Red D6 is a 2."

**GM:** "With a 6 on your gear die, you slice through the ICE like butter. The blast doors unseal with a heavy hiss and slide open. However, because you rolled a 4 and 5 on your skill dice—good, but not perfect stealth—the system logs a minor anomaly. I'm filling in one segment of the Corporate Alert clock." *(Alex shades in one slice of the paper clock. It is now at 1/8).*

**Chloe (Lyra):** "Let's move before they send a patrol to investigate the anomaly."

### Part 3: The Turret Room

**GM:** "You step into the antechamber. The blast doors seal shut behind you. The room is dark, but as you move forward, red targeting lasers cut through the gloom. Two automated turrets drop from the ceiling. Roll for initiative... Actually, no, Zero-Sum means we just go. Who's acting first?"

**Marcus (Kael):** "I am. I'm stepping in front of Jax and Lyra, spinning up my heavy rotary cannon. I'm going to lay down suppressive fire to give them cover to act. I'm spending 2 AP tokens for a massive burst." *(Marcus tosses two chips in. 1 AP left).*

**GM:** "You're drawing all the fire. Roll 3 Green D6s for your heavy weapons skill, and 1 Red D6 because you're standing in the open."

**Marcus (Kael):** "Green: 5, 6, 2. Red: 5."

**GM:** "A 6 means a critical success on the attack! You obliterate Turret 1. It sparks and explodes into pieces. But that Red 5 means the turret got a burst off before it died. Kael, you take a hit. Draw a physical Bleed Card."

*(Marcus reaches to the deck of Bleed Cards in the center of the table and flips the top one over).*

**Marcus (Kael):** "Ah, damn. 'Flesh Wound: Take -1 to Agility rolls until patched up. You are bleeding; if you stay in one location for too long, the biometric sensors will track your blood and tick the Alert clock.'"

**Chloe (Lyra):** "I'll handle Turret 2! I'm spending 1 AP." *(Tosses chip. 2 AP left).* "I'm deploying 'Arachne', my spider-drone. I want it to crawl along the ceiling, drop onto the turret, and unleash a localized EMP."

**GM:** "Love it. Roll 2 Green D6s for Piloting, 1 Blue D6 for the EMP gear. No Red dice because Kael is drawing the aggro."

**Chloe (Lyra):** "Green: 3 and 6. Blue: 4."

**GM:** "Clean success! Arachne drops down, a flash of blue electricity arcs across the room, and Turret 2 slumps downward, completely dead."

### Part 4: The Drone Bay & Control Room

**GM:** "With the antechamber clear, you kick open the doors to the central distribution bay. It's cavernous. Towering racks of synthetic rations stretch up into the darkness. Hundreds of cargo drones hang dormant on overhead rails. In the center is an elevated glass control booth."

**Sarah (Jax):** "I'm making a run for the control booth."

**GM:** "As you sprint toward it, the floor trembles. From behind a stack of shipping crates steps a 'Minotaur' class heavy security mech. It's nine feet tall, armed with dual chain-guns and a rocket pod. I'm drawing a new paper clock: 'Mech Charge'. It has 4 segments." *(Alex draws a 4-segment clock).* "When it fills, the mech is going to unleash a barrage that will level the room. Jax, you're at the terminal. What do you do?"

**Sarah (Jax):** "I need to hack this fast. I'm spending my last 2 AP tokens to brute-force the reprogramming." *(Sarah tosses her last two chips. 0 AP left).* "Rolling 3 Green D6s and 1 Red D6 for the mech's counter-intrusion software."

**Sarah (Jax):** "Oh no. Green: 1, 2, 3. Red: 6."

**GM:** "Ouch. The terminal flashes red. 'ACCESS DENIED. LOCKDOWN INITIATED.' The system actively fights back. You don't get through, and the alarm trips hard. I'm ticking the Corporate Alert clock up by 2 segments." *(Alert clock is now 3/8).* "The mech locks onto Lyra. It fires a warning salvo."

**Chloe (Lyra):** "I'm spending 1 AP to dodge and command my drone to distract it!" *(Tosses chip. 1 AP left).* "Rolling 2 Green D6s for Agility, 2 Red D6s for the incoming fire."

**Chloe (Lyra):** "Green: 4, 5. Red: 4, 5."

**GM:** "Barely. You dive behind a crate as bullets shred the plasteel where you were just standing. Kael, what are you doing?"

**Marcus (Kael):** "I'm still bleeding, but I can't stay still. I'll charge the mech! I'm spending my last AP." *(Tosses chip. 0 AP left).* "I'm using my rotary cannon like a battering ram, aiming for its knee joint. 4 Green D6s!"

**Marcus (Kael):** "Wow. 5, 5, 6, 6!"

**GM:** "Double crits! The table physically shakes as Kael slams into the mech. You shatter its knee actuator. The mech falls to one side, its targeting systems going haywire. I'll tick the 'Mech Charge' clock up by 1 because it's still active, but it's severely damaged."

**Sarah (Jax):** "Okay, I have 0 AP left. I have to rely on my base stats. I'm trying the hack again. Just 2 Green D6s, 1 Blue D6 from my gear, and 1 Red D6 for the system."

**GM:** "Roll it."

**Sarah (Jax):** "Green: 5, 6. Blue: 5. Red: 1."

**GM:** "Major success! The screen turns green. You're in. You override the distribution protocol."

### Part 5: The Climax & Escape

**GM:** "The entire warehouse hums to life. Hundreds of cargo drones detach from their rails, gripping crates of rations. The ceiling silos grind open, revealing the stormy night sky above. But your hack tripped the final failsafe. The Corporate Alert clock immediately jumps to 8/8." *(Alex shades the whole clock).* "Klaxons blare. Agri-Corp hit squads are dropping onto the roof. Furthermore, the dying mech initiates a self-destruct. I'm drawing a 3-segment clock." *(Alex draws a 3-segment clock).* "You have three actions before this whole place goes up."

**Chloe (Lyra):** "I spend my last AP to recall Arachne and sprint for the blast doors!" *(Tosses chip. 0 AP left).* "No roll needed for just running, right?"

**GM:** "Right, you and Jax make it to the antechamber. But Kael, you have that Bleed Card. 'Flesh Wound: -1 to Agility'. You're limping heavily, and you have no AP left."

**Marcus (Kael):** "I'm just going to push through the pain. I have to roll Agility to make it out in time, right?"

**GM:** "Yes. Base Agility is 2 Green D6s, but minus 1 from the Bleed Card, so just 1 Green D6. And 2 Red D6s because the room is literally exploding around you."

**Marcus (Kael):** "Come on... Green: 6! Red: 3, 4."

**GM:** "Yes! A 6! Kael, you dive through the blast doors at the absolute last second. The blast doors slam shut just as the mech detonates, the shockwave throwing you all out into the rainy street. You scramble into your escape van."

### Wrap Up

**GM:** "As your van speeds away into the neon-lit sprawl, you look out the window. High above Sector 4, hundreds of Agri-Corp cargo drones are swarming the sky. They open their bays, and millions of synthetic rations begin to rain down on the slums. The streets below flood with people cheering. You did it. Mama Z will be very pleased. End of session."

*(The players high-five across the table, exhausted but victorious).*
