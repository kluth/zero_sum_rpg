**<span style="font-size: 1.5em; float: left; margin-right: 5px;">I</span>n the shadows of megacities and corporate boardrooms, a new reality is dawning. Welcome to the ZERO SUM RPG.**

This is a hardcore tactical roleplaying experience where every victory comes at the direct expense of another. You are not a hero. You are an asset, a liability, a mercenary, or a ghost in the machine. 

As sovereign wealth syndicates bleed the world dry and rogue AI manipulates the global stock market, players navigate a brutal landscape of corporate espionage, high-stakes heists, and lethal encounters. The world does not care about you, and survival is entirely up to your tactical wit.

### 📰 FRONT PAGE NEWS: THE ZERO SUM CONSISTENCY MATRIX
In this universe, the mechanics are punishing but undeniably fair, governed by the ZSCM:
* **Lethality is High:** Firearms are deadly. Mistakes are fatal.
* **Resources are Scarce:** You enter with what you can carry; logistics are half the battle.
* **Intel is Asymmetric:** You never have the whole picture, but neither do your enemies.
* **Collateral Risk:** Every shot fired has economic and political consequences.

<br>
