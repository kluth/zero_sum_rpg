# ZERO SUM: Systemic Design Language & Styling Guide

## The Aesthetic: "Classified Corporate Dossier"
The design language for the Zero Sum RPG has been strictly defined to mirror the brutalist, paranoid, and hyper-corporate tone of the game's setting. The styling ensures the documents look like leaked internal corporate guidelines, threat-assessment files, or intercepted intelligence briefings.

### 1. Color Palette (Zero-Trust Minimalist)
* **Void Black (`#000000`):** Used for primary high-impact headers, table headers, and image borders. Represents the absolute, unyielding nature of the system.
* **Crimson Threat (`#d32f2f`):** The only vibrant color in the palette. Used for secondary headers, critical inline code, and the primary underline of H1 elements. Represents systemic violence, Heat, and Exhaust.
* **Concrete Grey (`#eaeaea` / `#f2f2f2`):** Used for blockquotes, alternating table rows, and backgrounds. Represents the bleak, sterile environments of corporate facilities.
* **Dossier White (`#f9f9f9`):** The base page color, slightly off-white to reduce eye strain and simulate physical intelligence reports.

### 2. Typography
* **Headers & UI Elements:** `Courier New` / Monospace. Simulates terminal outputs, code execution, and raw data feeds. Used for all H1, H2, table headers, and blockquotes.
* **Body Text:** `Inter` / `Helvetica Neue`. A modern, sterile, highly readable sans-serif font that represents corporate efficiency and lack of emotion.
* **Justification:** Body text is fully justified to create rigid, unbroken blocks of text, mirroring corporate documentation.

### 3. Structural Elements
* **Blockquotes (Intercepts):** Styled with a heavy black left border and a concrete grey background, rendered in Courier. These represent intercepted comms, raw intel, or flavor text.
* **Tables (Data Matrices):** Stark, brutalist tables with inverted black-and-white headers. No vertical lines, prioritizing lateral data reading.
* **Images:** All images are automatically processed through a CSS filter (`grayscale(100%) contrast(120%)`) to appear like surveillance footage or redacted black-and-white printouts.
* **Pagination & Watermarks:** Every page features a top-right marker: `ZERO SUM RPG // CONFIDENTIAL` to maintain the illusion of a leaked document.

This design language has been permanently applied to all PDF renders across the repository.
