The official Companion App for the analog tabletop RPG "Zero Sum: Blackout"!

Step into the shoes of an everyday hero in Zero Sum RPG: Blackout, a highly realistic crisis simulator. When a massive infrastructural collapse plunges the city into complete darkness, normal people—paramedics, IT technicians, journalists, and activists—must step up to survive and restore order.

This is not a fantasy. This is a grounded, desperate struggle for survival. Utilize the companion app featuring a groundbreaking, functional Neomorphic interface (Silk design) specifically built for field operations.

NEW IN THIS VERSION:
- Asymmetric Gameplay: Choose your role (Paramedic, Coordinator, Comms Officer) in the app. You only see your exclusive dashboard—enforcing real communication and coordination at the physical gaming table!
- Medical Dashboard: Monitor patient vitals with dynamic haptic ECG feedback.
- Heroic Dispatch Map: An interactive live map for the Coordinator to manage resources and incidents.
- Crisis Comm Center: Push-to-Talk radio featuring real static audio and asymmetric message feeds.
- Game Master Live-Integration: The Game Master can manipulate vitals, map markers, and radio messages live during the game via the Web Terminal, instantly updating the players' devices.

EXCLUSIVE LOCALIZED SCENARIOS (Inspired by True Events):
Play missions based on real historical crises and test if you could have done better:
- The Northeast Blackout (2003): 50 million people without power. Traffic lights dead, subways trapped. Can you prevent total anarchy?
- The Texas Power Crisis (Winter Storm Uri): The grid has failed under extreme freezing temperatures. Who gets the last backup generators?
- Hurricane Response: Communications are severed by extreme flooding. Use the Comms Radio module to coordinate makeshift rescue teams in the dark.

Can you lead your community through the darkest hours?
