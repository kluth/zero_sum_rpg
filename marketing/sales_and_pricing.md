# Zero Sum RPG - Monetization & Sales Strategy

## Executive Summary
Zero Sum RPG operates at the intersection of traditional tabletop roleplaying and digital app integration. To maximize adoption while covering ongoing Firebase backend costs, we must eliminate friction for the majority (the players) and extract value from the highly invested minority (the Game Masters). 

**The Core Decision:** The App is FREE for players. Game Masters (GMs) pay a one-time premium unlock. Rulebooks are sold primarily via PDF and Print-on-Demand (POD) to eliminate inventory risk.

---

## 1. App Monetization (Android / iOS)

### Decision: The "Free Player / Premium GM" Model
*   **Players:** The app is 100% free to download and use for joining campaigns. Players do not need an account or subscription to play. This removes the highest barrier to entry.
*   **Game Masters (GMs):** The app is free to download, but creating and hosting a campaign (which utilizes the Firebase real-time database) requires a one-time In-App Purchase (IAP) of **$14.99** for the "GM License". 

### Why not a Subscription Model?
While a subscription ($2.99/month) technically aligns better with ongoing Firebase cloud costs, the TTRPG community actively despises subscriptions for tools. A one-time GM License provides psychological safety for the buyer. 
*Firebase costs per session are fractions of a cent; a $14.99 one-time fee will cover the lifetime cloud database usage of a standard group with a massive margin.*

### Ongoing Revenue: Crisis Modules (DLC)
Instead of a subscription, we generate recurring revenue through **Crisis Modules (DLCs)**. The GM License includes the base scenario (e.g., "Grid Down: Urban Blackout"). Additional crises (e.g., "Category 5 Hurricane", "Cyberattack on Financial Infrastructure") will be sold via IAP for $4.99 to $9.99.

---

## 2. Rulebooks and Handouts: Digital vs. Physical

### Decision: DriveThruRPG PDF & Print-On-Demand (No Box Sets)
We will NOT print a physical box set upfront. Box sets require massive capital expenditure (Minimum Order Quantities of 1,000+ units, averaging $10-$15 per box in manufacturing, plus exorbitant international shipping and warehousing logistics).

Instead, we will utilize **DriveThruRPG** for zero-inventory distribution:

#### A. PDF Rulebook & Handout Pack ($14.99)
*   High-margin (DriveThruRPG takes ~30-35%).
*   Players/GMs receive the core rules and printable analog handouts (character sheets, crisis maps, emergency broadcast transcripts).
*   Zero distribution cost.

#### B. Print-on-Demand (POD) Rulebook ($29.99 Softcover / $39.99 Hardcover)
*   **Cost Breakdown:** Standard color printing for a ~150-page rulebook via DriveThruRPG costs ~$6.00 - $9.00 to print. 
*   Selling at $29.99 yields a gross profit of ~$21.00 per book (before the platform's 30% cut on the markup).
*   The customer pays for shipping directly, removing all fulfillment headaches from our team.

---

## 3. The Future: Kickstarter Box Set
Once the digital/POD ecosystem proves successful and builds a community, we will launch a **Kickstarter for a Premium Physical Box Set**. This box will contain the soft-touch Neomorphic UI aesthetic on physical ID cards, tactile crisis tokens, and high-quality map handouts. Crowdfunding shifts the financial risk to the consumer and funds the MOQ required for traditional manufacturing in China.

---

## Summary of Actionable Strategy
1. **App Stores:** List Zero Sum RPG as "Free". Implement IAP for GM Campaign Hosting ($14.99) and Expansion Scenarios ($4.99+).
2. **DriveThruRPG:** Release the PDF Rulebook ($14.99) and POD Physical Rulebook ($29.99+).
3. **Marketing:** Focus on the "Free to Play" aspect for groups. The GM buys the book and the app unlock; the players just download and play.
