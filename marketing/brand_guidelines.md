# Zero Sum RPG - Brand Guidelines

## 1. Core Theme: Everyday Hero Crisis Realism
The game's narrative and tabletop components strictly adhere to a highly realistic, gritty, and grounded aesthetic.
- **Theme:** "Everyday Hero" crisis simulator (e.g., urban blackout, disaster response).
- **Characters:** Normal people (Paramedic, IT Technician, Journalist, Activist). No superheroes, no magic.
- **Tone:** Gritty, desperate, realistic, dirty, and contemporary. 
- **Forbidden Elements:** No cyberpunk, no sci-fi, no fantasy, no glowing neon effects.

## 2. App UI Aesthetic: "Silk" Neomorphism
The digital companion app uses a pristine, soft UI style to contrast with the gritty reality of the game world.
- **Design System:** Neomorphic / Soft UI ("Stitch Silk").
- **Colors:** 
  - Primary: Indigo (`#6366f1`)
  - Background: Cool Gray (`#e8eaf0`)
  - Tertiary: Violet (`#7c3aed`)
- **Depth & Shadows:** Use raised and inset box-shadows to create "extruded light" tactile elements. Never use flat borders or gradients.
- **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans.

## 3. Marketing Assets
When creating marketing materials, blend the two core aesthetics:
- **Tabletop/Physical Components:** Emphasize the gritty reality. Use dark, moody lighting, dirt, and realistic urban decay.
- **App/Digital Components:** Highlight the clean, soft "Silk" neomorphic interface, providing a stark, high-tech (but contemporary) contrast to the physical world.
