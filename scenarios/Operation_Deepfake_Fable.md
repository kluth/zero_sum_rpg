This scenario was generated by the `zero-sum-scenario-generator` skill based on real-world news:
1. The restriction of the highly advanced "Fable" AI model due to jailbreak and national security concerns.
2. Massive 1.3 TB corporate data breaches by groups like ShinyHunters and FulcrumSec.

## Background
The advanced, restricted AI model code-named "FABLE-9" was stolen from a secure server farm by a data-broker cartel. They sold the weights to **"Viper"**, a radical, highly charismatic tech-influencer with over 40 million parasocial, fanatical followers. Viper is currently barricaded in a luxury penthouse in downtown Seattle.

Viper is using FABLE-9 to generate hyper-realistic, career-ending deepfakes of global politicians and rival influencers, holding them for ransom. If the ransom isn't paid, the deepfakes are broadcasted live to the 40 million followers.

## The Zero Sum Twist (The Hook)
The players are hired by a coalition of blackmailed victims to delete the FABLE-9 weights and neutralize Viper. However, Viper has weaponized her audience. Over 5,000 fanatic followers are currently camping outside her luxury high-rise, creating a massive, impenetrable human shield. If the players use traditional PMC tactics (guns, explosions), they will cause a civilian massacre, instantly raising their Heat Level to maximum and causing a permanent burnout.

## Zero Sum Consistency Matrix (ZSCM)
To ensure theoretical mathematical balance, this scenario is audited against the ZSCM matrix:
* **E (Lethality Expectation) = 4:** The followers are mostly unarmed, and Viper relies on digital defense. Direct firefights are rare but extremely dangerous if escalated.
* **R (Resource Scarcity) = 7:** Players cannot bring heavy weapons or tactical gear without alerting the civilian swarm. They must rely on concealed items and social engineering.
* **I (Intel Asymmetry) = 8:** Viper controls the building's digital infrastructure and the public narrative. She sees everything through her followers' smartphone cameras.
* **D (Collateral Damage Risk) = 10:** The highest possible rating. The "Dead Man's Zone" rule is constantly active. One misplaced flashbang or gunshot could trigger a deadly stampede or a mass doxxing attack.

**Total ZSCM Score = 29/30 (Thematically Hardcore, but Winnable).**

## Key NPCs
* **Viper (Digital Demagogue):** Charismatic, narcissistic, highly paranoid. She relies entirely on her "Swarm" for protection.
* **The Swarm (Environmental Hazard):** 5,000 civilians. They act as a massive sensory network. If they spot the players, they won't shoot—they will surround them, film them, dox them, and physically crush them.
* **Aegis Corporate Security:** The building's actual security force, who are overwhelmed by the followers and currently locked in the lobby, terrified of sparking a riot.

## Objective
1. Bypass the 5,000-person civilian swarm without triggering *Social Heat*.
2. Infiltrate the penthouse via unconventional means (e.g., BASE jumping from an adjacent building, or deep-cover social engineering).
3. Hack Viper's offline servers to delete FABLE-9 (requires extensive Downtime decryption to bypass the Zero-Trust Data logic bombs).
4. Extract without Viper directing the Swarm to tear you apart.
