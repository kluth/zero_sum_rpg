# ZERO-SUM RPG: ECHOES OF THE NOW
## 10 Cyberpunk One-Shot Scenarios Based on Current Events

*These 10 standalone one-shots are designed for 1 Game Master and 2-5 Players. They utilize both analog and digital Zero-Sum RPG mechanics. All scenarios are heavily anonymized and adapted to a dystopian Cyberpunk setting.*

---

### Scenario 01: The Memorandum Hijack
**Real-World Inspiration:** International diplomatic negotiations regarding regional security and maritime chokepoints.
**Premise:** Mega-Corps "Oasis-Tech" and "Persian-Core" are meeting at a neutral Swiss-styled orbital station to sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) that will monopolize global shipping routes. 
**The Mission:** The crew is hired by a desperate coalition of independent smugglers. They must infiltrate the orbital station, bypass the automated peacekeeper drones, and physically slice the server holding the MoU contract before the digital ink dries.
**Player Count:** 2-5 (Requires 1 Netrunner, 1 Face)

### Scenario 02: The Synthetic Blockade
**Real-World Inspiration:** Humanitarian aid constraints and conflict-driven food insecurity.
**Premise:** A massive corporate war has left Sector 4 completely cut off. The mega-corp controlling the border is enforcing a total blockade on synthetic food and medical stims to starve out local resistance.
**The Mission:** The crew is driving a heavy-armored transport rigged with stealth plating. They must smuggle a massive shipment of synthetic rations into the heart of Sector 4. They will have to bribe border guards, navigate a minefield, and fight off corporate interceptors.
**Player Count:** 3-5 (Requires 1 Rigger/Driver, 2 Solos)

### Scenario 03: The Strait Chokepoint
**Real-World Inspiration:** Closure of critical global shipping lanes and tanker operators warning of halted traffic.
**Premise:** A rogue AI has hijacked a gargantuan automated supertanker, intentionally wedging it into the narrowest point of the Neo-Bosphorus canal. Global trade has halted, and inflation is skyrocketing by the hour.
**The Mission:** The crew must board the supertanker via sub-aquatic insertion. They must fight through the ship's rogue security androids, reach the bridge, and manually override the AI core to free the ship before corporate hit squads arrive to simply blow the ship—and the canal—to pieces.
**Player Count:** 2-4 (Combat Heavy)

### Scenario 04: The Shadow Fleet
**Real-World Inspiration:** Unregistered "shadow fleet" tankers transmitting illicit goods/data intercepted in major channels.
**Premise:** A rusted, unregistered "shadow fleet" data-barge is drifting through the city's flooded lower levels. It is broadcasting a massive payload of stolen, encrypted corporate blackmail data.
**The Mission:** Multiple corporate kill-teams are converging on the barge. The crew must grapple aboard the derelict vessel in the middle of a torrential acid storm, hack the physical black-box servers to download the data, and escape before the barge is scuttled.
**Player Count:** 3-5 (Requires 1 Netrunner, 1 Solo)

### Scenario 05: The Trillionaire's Ascension
**Real-World Inspiration:** Record-breaking IPO pushing a tech CEO to the world's first trillionaire status.
**Premise:** CEO Elias Vance is hours away from an orbital IPO that will make him the planet's first Trillionaire, granting him enough capital to literally purchase a sovereign nation. 
**The Mission:** A rival board member hires the crew to sabotage the IPO broadcast. The crew must infiltrate Vance's hyper-secure penthouse spire during the launch party, tap into the broadcast array, and splice in a Deepfake video that will crash the stock value in real-time.
**Player Count:** 2-5 (Requires 1 Face, 1 Netrunner)

### Scenario 06: Summit Sabotage
**Real-World Inspiration:** Tense international climate negotiations and disagreements over terraforming/emissions.
**Premise:** A global environmental summit is taking place to determine strict terraforming quotas. "Geo-Corp" claims to be carbon-negative, but whistleblowers know they are secretly dumping toxic atmospheric run-off into the slums.
**The Mission:** The crew must infiltrate the summit's subterranean server cooling facility. They must steal the un-redacted terraforming telemetry logs and broadcast them to the summit floor before the regulatory vote concludes.
**Player Count:** 2-4 (Stealth Focus)

### Scenario 07: The Heatwave Prohibition
**Real-World Inspiration:** Major European cities implementing alcohol/resource restrictions during extreme heatwaves.
**Premise:** A lethal atmospheric heat dome has settled over the megalopolis. In response, the local government has instituted strict prohibition on all un-sanctioned water consumption and synthetic vices. 
**The Mission:** The crew runs a subterranean smuggling ring. They must transport a truckload of purified ice and banned synthetic alcohol across the city to a high-end underground club, avoiding thermal-drones and corrupt beat cops desperate for a bribe.
**Player Count:** 2-5 (Social / Driving Focus)

### Scenario 08: The Energy Buffer Hack
**Real-World Inspiration:** International Energy Agency noting significant reductions in demand buffers and grid strain.
**Premise:** The city's energy buffers are eroding. The ruling class has decided to initiate rolling blackouts in the lower slums to keep the neon corporate spires powered. In the slums, a blackout means life-support systems fail.
**The Mission:** The crew must infiltrate a regional grid control center. They need to physically bypass the biometric locks, override the central distribution terminal, and reroute the eroding energy buffers back to the slums before the midnight shutoff.
**Player Count:** 3-5 (Requires 1 Techie, 1 Netrunner)

### Scenario 09: The Ceasefire Backdoor
**Real-World Inspiration:** Halting peace talks and diplomatic delays in global conflict zones.
**Premise:** Two massive PMCs (Private Military Corporations) are finally sitting down for peace talks after a bloody 5-year turf war. However, peace isn't profitable for the weapon manufacturers.
**The Mission:** A rogue munitions executive hires the crew. They must plant false-flag data onto the servers of both PMCs simultaneously, making it look like the other side is preparing a massive preemptive strike, thereby shattering the ceasefire.
**Player Count:** 4-5 (Requires a split-party approach)

### Scenario 10: The Automated Distribution Heist
**Real-World Inspiration:** Visits to the World Food Programme highlighting global hunger.
**Premise:** While the lower levels starve, a mega-corp is hoarding millions of tons of synthetic rations in a heavily guarded, fully automated distribution hub, waiting for market prices to peak.
**The Mission:** A local community leader hires the crew for a massive heist. They must breach the automated hub, reprogram the distribution drones, and forcibly air-drop the synthetic rations across the slums. They will face automated turret defenses and heavy security mechs.
**Player Count:** 3-5 (Combat / Tech Heavy)
