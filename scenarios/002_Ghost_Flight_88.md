# Zero Sum RPG Scenario: 002 - Ghost Flight 88

![ghost_flight_monochrome](/home/matthias/.gemini/antigravity-cli/brain/886fd2e1-5ee6-43d8-868f-50c9af6b6a95/ghost_flight_monochrome_1781900358630.jpg)

## Real-World Inspiration
Based on the rise of autonomous drone cargo flights and GPS-spoofing incidents in commercial aviation.

## Background
A fully autonomous heavy-lift cargo plane (Flight 88) carrying a classified quantum-decryption prototype is crossing the Pacific. A rogue state actor has successfully GPS-spoofed the plane, causing it to alter course toward a black-site airstrip. The players must board the plane mid-flight from a stealth transport, regain manual control, and parachute the prototype out over international waters before the rogue state intercepts it.

## The Zero Sum Twist
There are no human pilots. The cockpit is sealed and filled with nitrogen to prevent fires. Furthermore, the plane's cargo hold is packed with high-explosive munitions. A single gunshot or stray spark in the cargo bay will obliterate the entire aircraft. The players must engage the rogue state's stowaway hijackers in absolute silence, using only non-lethal or kinetic melee weapons.

## Zero Sum Consistency Matrix (ZSCM)
* **E (Lethality Expectation) = 8:** If a gun is fired, everyone dies. The hijackers know this, creating a brutal hand-to-hand environment.
* **R (Resource Scarcity) = 9:** You are 30,000 feet in the air. There is no backup, no exfil, and no extra gear.
* **I (Intel Asymmetry) = 5:** The players have the plane's schematics, but the hijackers have root access to the plane's internal sensors.
* **D (Collateral Damage Risk) = 6:** A crash over the ocean minimizes civilian casualties, but guarantees total mission failure.

**Total ZSCM Score = 28/30 (Extremely Lethal).**

## Key NPCs & Obstacles
* **The "Spetsnaz" Stowaways:** Highly trained, utterly silent hand-to-hand combatants waiting in the dark cargo hold.
* **The Nitrogen Cockpit:** Breaking into the cockpit means dealing with an oxygen-deprived environment. Players have 60 seconds of breathable air unless they bring localized oxygen supplies.

## Objective
1. Board the plane mid-flight (requires high Kinetics/Piloting checks).
2. Neutralize the hijackers without using firearms.
3. Access the nitrogen-flooded cockpit to override the autopilot.
4. Drop the payload and HALO jump out before the plane enters hostile airspace.
