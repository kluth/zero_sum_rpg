# Items, Technology, and Logistics

In the modern world, information is a weapon, and your smartphone is a tracking device. Equipment in *Zero Sum* is about survival, staying off the grid, and gathering intelligence.

## Everyday Paranoia Gear
* **Burner Phones (Pre-paid):** Essential for communication. Must be discarded after a few uses or a specific operation. (Cost: Low. Availability: High).
* **Faraday Bags:** Pouches lined with metallic mesh that block all electromagnetic signals. Used to transport phones or laptops without being tracked via GPS, Wi-Fi, or cell towers.
* **Directional Microphones & Laser Mics:** Used for surveillance from a distance. Laser mics can read vibrations off glass windows to listen to conversations inside a room.
* **YubiKeys / Hardware Tokens:** Physical security keys required for accessing encrypted drives. If the players lose it, the data is gone forever.

## Cyber Warfare Tools
* **Stingray (IMSI Catcher):** A device the size of a suitcase that mimics a cell phone tower, forcing all nearby phones to connect to it. Used to intercept calls, texts, and track locations. Highly illegal, heavily used by law enforcement and private intelligence.
* **Zero-Click Exploits:** Software that can compromise a target's phone just by sending them a message (they don't even have to click a link). Grants full access to camera, microphone, and encrypted apps. Extremely rare and expensive.
* **Tails OS USB Drives:** An amnesic incognito operating system that boots from a USB stick and routes all traffic through the Tor network, leaving no trace on the host computer.

## Firearms and Violence
Guns are loud, leave forensic evidence, and escalate the Heat Level immediately.
* **Concealed Handguns (e.g., Glock 19, Sig P365):** The most common weapons. Easy to hide, but firing one without a suppressor guarantees a police response within minutes in an urban area.
* **Untraceable "Ghost Guns":** 3D-printed receivers combined with standard parts. No serial numbers. Expensive to acquire on the black market, but vital for deniable operations.
* **Psychological Burn Kits:** Since hospitals are required to report gunshot wounds, players must rely on military-grade psychological burn kits (tourniquets, QuikClot, chest seals) and underground doctors to survive.

## Financial Tools
* **Monero / Privacy Coins:** The only way to move money anonymously. Bitcoin is entirely traceable by forensic accountants.
* **Pre-paid Debit Cards (Straw Purchases):** Bought with cash by a third party (a "straw buyer") to pay for hotels, servers, or travel without linking to the player's identity.
